Small cap stocks are listed companies that have market capitalizations typically ranging from $300 million to $2 billion. Since the share prices of these companies can have big fluctuations over a short period of time, companies with market caps of up to $10 billion are also found in the small cap universe . Some of the stocks classified as small cap include casual-wear retailer Abercrombie & Fitch Co. ( ANF ), cloud computing business Fastly Inc. ( FSLY ), and global transportation company CAI International Inc. ( CAI ).
