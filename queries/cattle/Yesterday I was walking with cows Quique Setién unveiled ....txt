Quique Setién has been unveiled as the new coach of Barcelona. At his presentation Setién talked of a whirlwind 24 hours in which he went from walking with cows in the countryside to taking Barcelona's top job. 'Yesterday, I was walking around my home town with cows around me and now I’m here at Barcelona managing the best players in the world.' said the former Real Betis manager. The 61-year-old takes over from Ernesto Valverde and inherits a club that are top of La Liga and through to the knockout phase of the Champions League as group winners
