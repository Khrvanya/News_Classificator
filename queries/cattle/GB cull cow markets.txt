Average cull cow prices and throughputs at auction markets in Great Britain. This data is updated on a monthly basis.
The average liveweight price of the dairy sired cull cow for May 2020 was 107.08p/kg, up 2.11p/kg (2.0%) compared to May 2019.
During May 2020 the average liveweight beef sired cull cow price was 131.53p/kg, an increase of 1.91p/kg (1.5%) compared to May 2019.
Monthly figures are based on statistical months (January, April, July and October are 5 week months. The remainder are 4 week months)
