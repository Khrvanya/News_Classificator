What is the Digital Print Edition?
Read the paper anytime, anywhere with The Weekly Times, Rural Weekly and Tasmanian Country Digital Print Editions. This is an exact replica of the printed papers including all your favourite liftouts and magazines, with great digital features to enhance your experience.
How often is Digital Print Edition updated?
Just like the weekly paper, the Digital Print Edition is available every week that the paper is printed. The Weekly Times is available from 6am every Wednesday and Rural Weekly and Tasmanian Country are available from 6am every Friday.
The Digital Print Edition can be viewed on any device - desktop computer, tablet or smartphone.
Who gets to enjoy Digital Membership benefits?
Do you have a Paper Home Delivery subscription with access to the Digital Print Edition or a standalone Digital subscription? If so, then you're already a Member and can enjoy unrestricted digital access at theweeklytimes.com.au.
Do you have a Paper Home Delivery only subscription? If so, we are inviting you to upgrade your subscription to include digital access for free. If you would like to upgrade your subscription to include Digital please call us on 1300 MY NEWS (1300 696 397), Monday to Friday 7.30am to 6.00pm and 7.00am - 11.30am AEST on both Saturdays and Sundays.
Why should I pay for a Digital Membership when I can access news for free on other sites?
The answer is simple. Only Digital Members enjoy unrestricted access to stories, insight and analysis from Australia's best rural journalists, across your favourite devices.
