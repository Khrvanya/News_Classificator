Poland received EUR 1.59 billion from EU funds in May 2020, including EUR 779 million in current transfers and EUR 808 million in capital transfers, the Finance Ministry said in a statement on Tuesday.
Poland obtained EUR 868 million within the framework of Cohesion Policy and EUR 678 million from the Common Agricultural Policy budget in May, the data showed.
Poland, in turn, paid EUR 664 million into the EU budget in May, the ministry said.
During the first five months of the year, Poland received a total of EUR 9.66 billion in EU funds and paid EUR 2.75 billion to the EU.
