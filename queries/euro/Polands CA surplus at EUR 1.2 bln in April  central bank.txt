Poland recorded a EUR 1.163 billion current account surplus in April 2020, against a EUR 1.070 billion current account surplus market consensus, according to National Bank of Poland (NBP) figures released on Monday.
Poland recorded a EUR 1.809-billion surplus in services, a EUR 85-million deficit in primary income and a EUR 26-million surplus in secondary income.
It also recorded a EUR 33-million deficit in trade in goods.
Exports of goods in March fell by 29.2 percent year on year and imports went down by 28.8 percent.
