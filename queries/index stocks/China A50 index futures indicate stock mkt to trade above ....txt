Jun 17, 2020 (China Knowledge) - BJT 09:01 - FTSE China A50 opens at 13,573. The index remains relatively unchanged from the previous close. The index is 7.5 points higher or up 0.06% than the previous close.
Yesterday the Shanghai Composite Index closed at 2,931.75, up 1.44%, and Shenzhen Component Index finished at 11,398.97, up 1.85%, the Hang Seng Index closed at 24,344.09, up 2.39%. The trading volume for three exchanges recorded USD 116 bln, down 6.75% from the previous trading day.
The markets turned up 3,258 gainers, 494 losers, and 83 remained unchanged. There were 87 triggered the limit up 10% ‘circuit breakers’ and 13 hit the limit down 10% cap.
Dow Jones closed at 26,289.98, and the NASDAQ closed at 9,895.87 last night.
