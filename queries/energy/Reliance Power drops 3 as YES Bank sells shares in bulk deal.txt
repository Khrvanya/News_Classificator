Shares of Anil Ambani Group's Reliance Power NSE 4.71 % dropped 3 per cent in Wednesdayâ€™s trade after YES Bank NSE 5.08 % sold over 3 crore shares in an open market transaction on Tuesday.
According to bulk deal data on the National Stock Exchange (NSE), the private lender sold a total of 3,02,59,855 shares, representing 1.07 per cent of total shareholding in Reliance Power.
The bank sold the shares at an average price of Rs 1.91 apiece, which took the total deal value to over Rs 5.77 crore. The buyer, however, could not be ascertained.
Shares of Reliance Power were quoting at Rs 1.82, down 3.19 per cent on BSE. The stock is one of the biggest wealth destroyers in the last one year, down 93 per cent.
Meanwhile, YES Bank shares drifted 0.12 per cent to Rs 41.60. The scrip is now down 79 per cent in last one year.
