Chesapeake Energy is on the ropes again, and this time it might not be able to skirt bankruptcy.
The Oklahoma oil-and-gas producer missed a $10 million debt payment on Monday and is now in the midst of a grace period before its creditors potentially take action. The Wall Street Journal reported that the company is preparing to file for bankruptcy protection as soon as next Monday . The company declined to comment.
