The chief financial officer of North Sea oil and gas explorer Hurricane Energy has resigned.
Alistair Stobie, who joined in 2016, has stepped down for the board with immediate effect to be replaced by head of finance Richard Chaffe on an acting basis.
Chaffe joined in 2016 having previously been finance director at EOG Resources in the UK and worked for Ernst & Young.
