SYRACUSE, N.Y. - A crowd nearing 300 people gathered in the parking lot behind the Syracuse Post-Standard printing press to support Black Lives Matter. The peaceful group led by Black Cuse Pride and CNY Pride marched to city hall where they held a rally.
Becoming emotional a leader from Black Cuse Pride noted that the pride flag being raised at city hall included brown and black stripes for the first time.
