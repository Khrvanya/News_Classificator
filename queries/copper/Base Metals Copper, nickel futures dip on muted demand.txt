New Delhi: Copper futures on Friday traded 0.44 per cent lower at Rs 429.10 per kg after participants offloaded their positions on weak spot demand.
On the Multi Commodity Exchange, copper contracts for March delivery fell by Rs 1.90, or 0.44 per cent, to Rs 429.10 per kg in a business turnover of 2,374 lots.
Similarly, copper contracts for April delivery traded lower by Rs 2.35, or 0.54 per cent, to Rs 431.25 per kg in a business turnover of 22 lots.
Traders said subdued demand in spot market mainly pushed down copper prices here.
Nickel prices on Friday fell by Rs 11 to Rs 948.30 per kg in futures trade after participants trimmed their positions amid weak demand at spot market.
On the Multi Commodity Exchange, nickel contracts for March delivery fell by Rs 11, or 1.15 per cent, to Rs 948.30 per kg with a business turnover of 1,726 lots.
Similarly, nickel contracts for April delivery dropped by Rs 9.30, or 0.97 per cent, to Rs 951.20 per kg in 12 lots.
Analysts said subdued demand at spot market mainly led to fall in nickel prices here.
