New Delhi, Mar 13 () Copper futures on Friday traded 0.59 per cent lower at Rs 419.60 per kg after participants offloaded their positions on weak spot demand.
On the Multi Commodity Exchange, copper contracts for March delivery fell by Rs 2.50, or 0.59 per cent, to Rs 419.60 per kg in a business turnover of 4,422 lots.
Similarly, copper contracts for April delivery traded lower by Rs 1.95, or 0.46 per cent, to Rs 423.30 per kg in a business turnover of 253 lots.
Traders said subdued demand in spot market mainly pushed down copper prices here. Advertisement
