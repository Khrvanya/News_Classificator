NEW DELHI: Most of the metal stocks were trading with gains in Monday's afternoon session.
Shares of Hindustan Copper (up 4.78 per cent), APL Apollo Tubes (up 3.62 per cent), VEDL (up 1.91 per cent), Hindustan Zinc (up 0.94 per cent) and JSW Steel (up 0.85 per cent) were the top gainers.
Tata Steel (up 0.67 per cent), Jindal Steel & Power (up 0.6 per cent), Ratnamani Metals & Tubes (up 0.56 per cent) and National Mineral Development Corp (up 0.52 per cent) too were trading with gains.
While Welspun Corp (down 1.95 per cent) and MOIL (down 1.46 per cent) were the top losers in the index.
The Nifty Metal index was trading 0.77 per cent up at 2,728.45 around 11:55 am.
Benchmark NSE Nifty50 index was up 1.90 points at 12,273.7, while the BSE Sensex was 28.01 points at 41,653.53.
Among the 50 stocks in the Nifty index, 34 were trading in the green, while 16 were in the red.
Shares of YES Bank, Vodafone Idea, Tata Motors, Indiabulls Housing, SAIL, SBI, JSPL, Zee Entertainment, Bank of Baroda and Tata Steel were among the most traded shares on the NSE.
