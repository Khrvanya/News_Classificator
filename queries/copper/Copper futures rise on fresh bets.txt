On the Multi Commodity Exchange, copper contracts for delivery in May traded higher by Re 1, or 0.24 percent, at Rs 412.15 per kg in a business turnover of 3,604 lots.
Copper prices on Wednesday traded 0.24 percent higher at Rs 412.15 per kg in futures market on the back of pick-up in spot demand.
On the Multi Commodity Exchange, copper contracts for delivery in May traded higher by Re 1, or 0.24 percent, at Rs 412.15 per kg in a business turnover of 3,604 lots.
Analysts attributed the rise in copper prices to raising of bets by participants driven by pick-up in spot demand.
