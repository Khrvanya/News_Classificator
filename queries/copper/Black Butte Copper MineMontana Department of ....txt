MDEQ is stated to have approved Tintina Montana Inc.’s (“Tintina”) permit application for the copper mine. It is described as a copper mining project proposed in the Smith River watershed of west-central Montana.
The Complaint describes the proposed mining activities as including excavation of approximately 440 tons of concentrated copper ore on a daily basis in the watershed. It states that over the life of the project, the mining activities would generated an estimated 12.9 million tons of tailings and nearly one million tons of waste rock.
The Complaint alleges the violation of certain Montana statues and environmental regulations in the issuance of a permit.
