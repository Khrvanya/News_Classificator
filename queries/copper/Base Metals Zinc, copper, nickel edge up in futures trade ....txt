Aluminium prices fell by 35 paise at Rs 135.45 per kg in futures trade on Wednesday as participants trimmed positions, tracking a weak trend at spot market on sluggish demand.
On the Multi Commodity Exchange, aluminium for October delivery eased by 35 paise, or 0.26 per cent, to Rs 135.45 per kg in a business turnover of 271 lots.
Similarly, the metal for November delivery weakened by 25 paise, or 0.18 per cent, to Rs 137.80 per kg in 10 lots.
Analysts attributed the weakness in aluminium prices to slackened demand from consuming industries in the spot market.
