New Delhi: Nickel prices on Friday gained 0.97 per cent to Rs 917.50 per kg in futures trade as speculators built positions on rising demand from alloy-makers in spot market.
On the Multi Commodity Exchange, nickel contracts for April delivery gained Rs 8.80, or 0.97 per cent, to Rs 917.50 per kg in a business turnover of 1,180 lots.
Similarly, the metal for May delivery traded higher by Rs 9.70, or 1.07 per cent, at Rs 913.10 per kg in a business turnover of 107 lots.
Rising demand in spot market from alloy-makers mainly supported price rise in nickel futures, analysts said.
Copper prices on Friday traded up 1.32 per cent at Rs 403.20 per kg in futures market on the back of pick-up in spot demand.
On the Multi Commodity Exchange, copper contracts for delivery in April traded higher by Rs 5.25, or 1.32 per cent, at Rs 403.20 per kg in a business turnover of 3,104 lots.
Similarly, the metal for delivery in May edged up by Rs 5.35, or 1.33 per cent, to Rs 406.30 per kg in 410 lots.
Analysts attributed the rise in copper prices to raising of bets by participants driven by pick-up in spot demand.
