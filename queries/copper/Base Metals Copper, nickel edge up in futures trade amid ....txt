Nickel prices on Friday edged up by Rs 4 to Rs 943.40 per kg in futures trade as speculators built positions on rising demand from alloy-makers at spot market.
On the Multi Commodity Exchange, nickel contracts for February delivery gained Rs 4, or 0.43 per cent, to Rs 943.40 per kg in a business turnover of 2,185 lots.
Similarly, the metal for March delivery traded higher by Rs 4.80, or 0.51 per cent, at Rs 941.70 per kg in a business turnover of 15 lots.
Rising demand in spot market from alloy-makers supported price rise in nickel futures , analysts said.
Copper prices on Friday traded up by Rs 1.70 at Rs 428.20 per kg in futures market on the back of pick-up in spot demand.
On the Multi Commodity Exchange, copper contracts for delivery in February traded higher by Rs 1.70, or 0.40 per cent, at Rs 428.20 per kg in a business turnover of 2,177 lots.
Similarly, the metal for delivery in March edged up by Rs 1.60, or 0.37 per cent, to Rs 429.90 per kg in 30 lots.
Analysts attributed the rise in copper prices to raising of bets by participants driven by a pick-up in spot demand.
