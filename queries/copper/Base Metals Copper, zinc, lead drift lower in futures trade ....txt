Nickel prices drifted lower by Rs 7.10 to Rs 1,229.60 per kg in futures trade on Wednesday as speculators trimmed their positions owing to weak demand at the spot market.
At the Multi Commodity Exchange , nickel for delivery in October declined by Rs 7.10, or 0.57 per cent, to Rs 1,229.60 per kg in a business turnover of 6,158 lots.
Similarly, the metal for delivery in November dropped by Rs 4.70, or 0.38 per cent, to Rs 1,223.50 per kg in 276 lots.
Muted demand from alloy makers in spot market mainly weighed on nickel future prices, analysts said.
Lead prices fell by 40 paise at Rs 155.40 per kg in futures market on Wednesday as speculators cut bets, taking negative cues from the spot market.
On the Multi Commodity Exchange, lead for delivery in October fell by 40 paise, or 0.26 per cent, at Rs 155.40 per kg in a business turnover of 1,552 lots.
The metal for delivery in November shed 36 paise, or 0.22 per cent, at Rs 155.90 per kg in 18 lots.
Analysts said off-loading of positions by participants owing to slackened demand from consuming industries in physical market, mainly influenced lead prices at futures trade.
