New Delhi: Nickel prices on Thursday fell 0.49 per cent to Rs 853 per kg in futures trade after participants trimmed their positions amid weak demand at spot market.
On the Multi Commodity Exchange, nickel contracts for March delivery ease by Rs 4.20, or 0.49 per cent, to Rs 853 per kg with a business turnover of 9,260 lots.
Similarly, nickel contracts for April delivery dropped by Rs 10.10, or 1.19 per cent, to Rs 841 per kg in 913 lots.
Analysts said subdued demand in spot market mainly led to fall in nickel prices here.
Copper futures on Thursday fell 5.15 per cent at Rs 362.10 per kg as speculators reduced their exposure on weak spot demand.
On the Multi Commodity Exchange, copper contracts for March delivery traded down by Rs 19.65, or 5.15 per cent, to Rs 362.10 per kg in a business turnover of 7,325 lots.
Similarly, copper contracts for April delivery traded lower by Rs 19.64, or 5.12 per cent, to Rs 363.90 per kg in a business turnover of 1,214 lots.
Traders said subdued demand in spot market mainly pushed down copper prices here.
