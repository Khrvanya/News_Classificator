New Delhi, Jun 25 (PTI) Silver futures on Thursday dropped by Rs 225 to Rs 47,561 per kg as participants reduced their bets on low demand.
On the Multi Commodity Exchange, silver contracts for July delivery tumbled by Rs 225, or 0.47 per cent, to Rs 47,561 per kg in a business turnover of 3,916 lots.
Similarly, silver for September delivery fell by Rs 184, or 0.38 per cent, to Rs 48,550 per kg in a business turnover of 4,264 lots.
Globally, silver traded lower by 0.20 per cent at USD 17.77 per ounce in New York. PTI  SHW RUJ
