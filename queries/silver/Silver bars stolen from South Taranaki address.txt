Police are asking gold and silver distributors to keep an eye out for a number of stolen silver ingots. (file photo)
A number of silver bars have recently been stolen from a South Taranaki address.
A post on a police Facebook page has asked gold and silver distributors in the wider Taranaki area to keep an eye out, as it is believed the stolen ingots or bullions are being sold.
Brokerage website www.goldbroker.com has the value of a 1kg silver bar at $926.58.
The bars are approximately 6cm long and 2.5cm wide and were packaged in clear, plastic sheeting, the post said.
Anyone with information can call 105, quoting file number 200613/1478, or information can be reported anonymously to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
