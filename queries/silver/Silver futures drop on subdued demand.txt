New Delhi, Jul 2 (PTI) Silver futures on Thursday dropped by Rs 60 to Rs 49,364 per kg as participants reduced their bets on low demand.
On the Multi Commodity Exchange, silver contracts for September delivery tumbled by Rs 60, or 0.12 per cent, to Rs 49,364 per kg in a business turnover of 10,323 lots.
Globally, silver traded lower by 0.02 per cent at USD 18.22 per ounce in New York. PTI SHW RUJ RUJ
