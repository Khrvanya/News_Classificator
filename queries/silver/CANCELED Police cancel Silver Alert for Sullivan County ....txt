FARMERSBURG, Ind. (WPTA21) - Police canceled a state wide silver alert issued for a Sullivan County teenager.
Authorities have issued a Silver Alert for a missing 16-year-old girl from Farmersburg, which is 84 miles southwest of Indianapolis.
The Sullivan County Sheriff’s Department is investigating the disappearance of Analiese Marie Pinskton, a 16-year-old white female, 4 feet 10 inches, 120 pounds, with brown hair and green eyes.
She was last seen wearing a dark black colored sleeveless top, white jeans and flip flops.
Pinskton is believed to be in the company of Collin Wayne Kelty, a 21-year-old white male, 6 feet 3 inches, 212 pounds, with brown hair and blue eyes.
She was last seen getting into a white 2014 Land Rover with Indiana license plate 902RQP.
Pinskton was last seen on Friday, June 26, 2020 at 4:00 pm and is believed to be in danger.
If you have any information on Analiese Marie Pinkston, contact the Sullivan County Sheriff’s Department at 812-268-4308 or 911.
