NEW LISBON, Wis. —  The Wisconsin Department of Justice has canceled a Silver Alert for an elderly man from New Lisbon.
Clifford Larson, 90, was found safe Wednesday morning.
He told family he was going to drive to his property in Iron Mountain, Michigan.
His family said he hasn’t driven in months due to cognitive and physical health issues and was concerned.
