A Glencoe man was arrested after a crash in Silver Lake Saturday evening.
At 7:16pm, the McLeod County Sheriff’s Office responded to a crash in the area of Gehlen Dr and Grove Ave SE in Silver Lake.
The Sheriff’s Office says the vehicle, a 2014 Chrysler, is owned and was being driven by 31-year-old Silvestre Lopez of Glencoe.
Lopez was transported to the Glencoe Hospital where he was treated for non-life-threatening injuries and released.
Authorities say he was then arrested for probable cause DWI and transported to the McLeod County Jail in Glencoe.
