Syracuse, N.Y. — Galaxy Media is bringing back its Moonlight Movie Series, but it won’t be held at the St. Joseph’s Health Amphitheater at Lakeview this year.
The outdoor movie series will instead move to the Finger Lakes Drive-In in Auburn for the 2020 season to ensure social distancing amid the coronavirus pandemic. A double-feature of film favorites will be shown each Wednesday, from July 1 to Aug. 26; gates open at 7:45 p.m. and movies start at 9 p.m.
Admission is $10 per carload; presale tickets are available online at www.moonlightmoviescny.com . Concessions will also be available.
