School districts around the state have tabulated ballots for their budget votes, with the vast majority passing.
Voting took place by absentee ballot due to the pandemic, with results delayed a week to allow for all votes to come in. The budget votes typically happen in May, but uncertainty with state funding and complications due to the coronavirus pandemic pushed that date back.
