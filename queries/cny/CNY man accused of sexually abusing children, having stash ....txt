CNY man accused of sexually abusing children, having stash of child porn
Clay, N.Y. — A Central New York man has been charged with sexually abusing two children and having a large stash of child pornography.
Richard Conto, 46, of Clay, was arrested Friday by detectives with the Onondaga County Internet Crimes Against Children Task Force. Detectives started investigating Conto after getting a tip from the New York ICAC Task Force, said Sgt. Jon Seeber, sheriff’s office spokesman.
Detectives discovered Conto had over 1,000 photos and videos of children being sexually abused, Seeber said. They also determined he had sexually abused two children — both under 14 years old — in 2006 and 2008, he said.
