Readers are invited to submit a photo showing the height of Jackson County corn on the Fourth of July for a chance to appear in the Jackson County Pilot and Lakefield Standard.
Submitted photos must be taken on July 4 and include at least one person standing next to the stalks. Submissions must include the names of those in the photo and the location of the cornfield.
Email high-resolution digital photos to editor@livewireprinting.com or drop them off at the offices of Livewire Printing Co. in downtown Jackson or the Lakefield Standard in downtown Lakefield by noon on Monday, July 6.
