James “Posey” Flowers, age 67, of Homer City died June 18, 2020 at Indiana Regional medical Center.  Posey was born on February 25, 1953 in Latrobe, PA and was the son of Catherine Flowers.
Posey was a Vietnam Veteran serving in the United States Army.  He was a life member of Coral Graceton Volunteer Fire Company, member of the Indiana County Hazmat Team 900 and a life member of the AMVETS.  He was also a member of the American Legion Post 493, Homer City and the Graceton-Coral Sportmans Club.
Posey enjoyed fishing, Nascar and the Pittsburgh Steelers and occasional casino with his wife.
Posey is survived by his wife of 18 years, Julie (Foor) Flowers.  He is also survived by step-children, Amy Rankin, Homer City; Ashley Sutter, Dubois and Fred Bugay Jr., Midland, TX.; and his four grandchildren.  Surviving siblings, Bill Garris and wife Nanette, Blairsville; Joni Gatlin and husband Daryl, Arkansas and Lori Everett.  Posey is also survived by his in-laws, Daryl and Kathryn Foor of New Enterprise, PA; 1 brother-in-law, Don Foor and wife Kelly, New Enterprise and 2 sister-in-laws, Carol Geiger and husband Ross of Philadelphia and Renae Campbell and husband Mark, Hollidaysburg, PA. Numerous nieces and nephews; great nieces and nephews also survive him.  He will be deeply missed by his 2 beloved dogs, Bentley and Mercedes.
Posey was preceded in death by his mother, four brothers, Tim Garris, Larry Harris and John Garris and his first wife, Denise Rankin Flowers.
Friends will be received Sunday, from 1-4PM with services at 4PM in the funeral home chapel at Bowser Funeral Home and Cremation Services, Inc.
