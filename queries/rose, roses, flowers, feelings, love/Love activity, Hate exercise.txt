As a member, you can help us tackle physical inactivity.
Physical inactivity is a major public health problem and something that CSP members want to tackle through our campaign ‘Love activity, Hate exercise?'
Our campaign draws on insight from patients and members across the profession, including physiotherapists with expertise in physical activity and behaviour change, to help identify barriers that prevent people from being more active.
