A family-owned Bradford business has pledged to donate £100,000 to NHS Charities Together.
Astonish, which has a factory on Staithgate Lane at Bradford , will donate profits from sales of a 'limited edition' lockdown-inspired anti-bacterial spray.
The 250,000 bottles feature the message ‘Spread Love Not Germs’. They are going on the shelves this week.
Howard Moss, Astonish managing director, said: "Since the start of this pandemic we have been doing what we can to help frontline workers.
A spokesman said Astonish would be donating a minimum of £100,000 to NHS Charities Together.
Astonish is a second-generation family-run business. The firm employs 85 people and produces more than 250,000 products every day.
