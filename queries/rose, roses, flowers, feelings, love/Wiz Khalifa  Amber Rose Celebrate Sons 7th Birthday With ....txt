Wiz Khalifa reunited with his ex-wife Amber Rose to celebrate their son Sebastian's 7th birthday over the weekend.
Rose, who is expecting a child with Def Jam A&R vp Alexander "AE" Edwards, shared several photos on social media from the birthday celebration, including a shot of Sebastian's creepy Pennywise-themed cake.
"Happy early Birthday to my Smart, Articulate, Sensitive, Compassionate Baby Boy Sebastian!" Rose captioned the Instagram post on Sunday (Feb. 16).
"He turns 7 on February 21st! He is so Blessed to have Amazing Family and Friends that Love him so much! WE LOVE YOU PUMPKIN!!!"
Sebastian dressed up as the It villain for Halloween, according to People . The birthday celebration was reportedly held at an indoors activities center in Thousand Oaks, Calif.
Rose shares Sebastian Taylor Thomaz with Wiz Khalifa. She filed for divorce from the rapper in 2014, citing irreconcilable differences.
Rose and Edwards have been dating since late last year and their debut as a couple at Rose's 4th annual SlutWalk in October 2018.
