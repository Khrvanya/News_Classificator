Jun 15, 2020 (China Knowledge) - BJT 09:01 - FTSE China A50 opens at 13,550. The index drops into negative territory. The index is 107.5 points lower or down 0.79% than the previous close.

Dow Jones closed at 25,605.54, and the NASDAQ closed at 9,588.81 last Friday.
