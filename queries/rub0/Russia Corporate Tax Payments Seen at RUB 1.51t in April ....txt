Russia Corporate Tax Payments Seen at RUB 1.51t in April: Survey
Mineral extraction tax payment seen at 243b rubles ($3.3b), while excise is forecast at 91b rubles and VAT predicted to be 320b rubles all due April 27, according to a survey of 7 banks and funds.
Surveyed banks and funds are: ING, Freedom Finance, Promsvyazbank, Uralsib, RDIF, Bank St. Petersburg, Nordea Bank
