Flame Tree Barbecue, located in Disney’s Animal Kingdom, is one of the more popular eateries on Walt Disney World property.
Disney guests can enjoy the delicious flavors of an assortment of smokehouse meat and chicken dinners, which are all served alongside Flame Tree Barbecue’s signature baked beans and coleslaw.
And now, you can make either the BBQ sauce or BBQ rub (or both) in the comfort of your own home thanks to these Disney recipes from The Ultimate Disney World Cookbook by David Kennedy.
Put these on a delicious burger, a slab of ribs, or other barbecue food items for a delicious Disney flavor — guaranteed to spice up your dinner.
