New Delhi: Crude oil futures on Friday rose 1.24 per cent to Rs 1,305 per barrel as participants widened their positions on firm spot demand..
On the Multi Commodity Exchange, crude oil for May delivery climbed by Rs 16, or 1.24 per cent, to Rs 1,305 per barrel with a business volume of 12,734 lots.
Analysts said raising of bets by participants kept crude prices higher in futures trade here.
Globally, West Texas Intermediate crude oil fell 4.61 per cent to $15.74 per barrel and Brent Crude slipped 2.72 per cent to $20.75 per barrel in New York.
