Natural gas is a ‘caveat’ in energy transition, EU admits
"The use of natural gas will probably be necessary to shift from coal to sustainable energy. I think this is a caveat I have to add,” said Frans Timmermans, the Commission's executive vice-president in charge of the European Green Deal. [ European Union, 2020. Source: EC - Audiovisual Service ]
