MOSCOW, 5 June (BelTA) – The Russian Energy Ministry has been instructed to address Belarusian concerns regarding shipments of natural gas. State Secretary of the Union State of Belarus and Russia Grigory Rapota made the statement after a session of the High Level Group of the Union State Council of Ministers on 5 June, BelTA has learned.
The official said: “The matter of tariffs on natural gas for Belarus was raised. The energy ministries are working on it now. They've been instructed to address Belarusian concerns. In order to find some solution that satisfies both sides.”
