New Delhi: Shares of Oil And Natural Gas Corporation NSE 0.31 % (ONGC) traded 1.3 per cent up in Thursday's trade at 12:36 pm. Around 1,80,17,355 shares changed hands on the counter. The stock opened at Rs 61.85 and touched an intraday high and low of Rs 62.65 and Rs 59.65, respectively, in the session so far.
The oil firm, which produced 64.5 million standard cubic meters of gas per day till earlier this week, has reduced the flow to 59.8 mmscmd on Wednesday, and will further cut by another 3 mmscmd on Thursday, sources aware of the development said.
The stock quoted a 52-week high of Rs 183.5 and a 52-week low of Rs 58.15.
Oil And Natural Gas Corporation, incorporated in the year 1993, is a Large Cap company (having a market cap of Rs 77368.72 crore) operating in Gas & Petroleum sector.
The company reported net profit after tax of Rs 4898.68 crore for the quarter ended 31 December, 2019. For the same quarter, the company has reported a Consolidated sales of Rs 109443.39 crore, up 7.77 per cent from last quarter Sales of Rs 101554.26 crore and down 6.70 per cent from last year same quarter Sales of Rs 117305.80 crore.
