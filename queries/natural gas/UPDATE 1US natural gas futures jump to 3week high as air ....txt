    May 29 (Reuters) - U.S. natural gas futures rose to a three-week high on Friday on forecasts for
warmer weather and higher air conditioning demand over the next two weeks.
    Front-month gas futures for July delivery rose 2.2 cents, or 1.2%, to settle at $1.849 per
million British thermal units, their highest close since May 7.

was down about 6% after jumping by a 17-month high of 19% in April.
