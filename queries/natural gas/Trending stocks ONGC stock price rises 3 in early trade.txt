NEW DELHI: Shares of Oil And Natural Gas Corporation traded 2.66 per cent up in Friday 's trade at 10:10 am.
Around 8454800 shares changed hands on the counter.
The stock opened at Rs 69.0 and touched an intraday high and low of Rs 69.5 and Rs 67.15 , respectively, in the session so far.
The state-owned firm will lose about Rs 4,000 crore in revenue and start making cash losses after the government slashed the natural gas prices by a steep 26 per cent by benchmarking it against rates prevalent in gas-surplus nations.
Oil And Natural Gas Corporation Ltd., incorporated in the year 1993, is a Large Cap company (having a market cap of Rs 82715.34 Crore) operating in Gas & Petroleum sector.
