Soybean prices on Friday fell by Rs 28 to Rs 4,360 per quintal in futures market as traders cut down their positions amid weak cues from physical markets.
On the National Commodity and Derivatives Exchange, soybean to be delivered in January contracts moved down by Rs 28, or 0.64 per cent, to Rs 4,360 per quintal with an open interest of 90,830 lots.
Soybean for delivery in February contracts also eased by Rs 24, or 0.54 per cent, to Rs 4,380 per quintal with an open interest of 1,76,510 lots.
The fall in soybean prices was mostly due to reduction of exposure by participants, marketmen said.
Refined soya oil prices on Friday fell by Rs 8.8 to Rs 937 per 10 kg in futures trade as speculators reduced holdings amid subdued demand in spot market.
On the National Commodity and Derivatives Exchange, refined soya oil for delivery in January declined by Rs 8.8, or 0.93 per cent, to Rs 937 per 10 kg in 31,430 lots.
The refined soya oil contracts for February dropped by Rs 9.4, or 0.99 per cent, to Rs 935.6 per 10 kg in 53,570 lots.
Market analysts said cutting down of positions by participants against ample stocks mainly influenced prices.
Mustard seed prices on Friday dropped by Rs 28 to Rs 4,619 per quintal in futures trade as speculators cut down their positions on subdued demand.
On the National Commodity and Derivatives Exchange, mustard seed contracts for January delivery fell by Rs 28, or 0.60 per cent, to Rs 4,619 per quintal in a business turnover of 15,350 lots.
Mustard seed contracts for February delivery declined by Rs 34, or 0.75 per cent, to Rs 4,620 per quintal in a business turnover of 9,050 lots.
Marketmen said offloading of positions by participants following a weak trend in spot market led to fall in mustard seed prices.
Guar seed prices on Friday fell by Rs 56 to Rs 4,154 per 10 quintal in futures trade as participants reduced their holdings in line with weak market trend.
Marketmen attributed the downward trend in guar seed futures prices to increasing supplies from growing regions.
On the National Commodity and Derivatives Exchange, guar seed contracts for January delivery fell by Rs 56, or 1.33 per cent, to Rs 4,154 per 10 quintal with an open interest of 47,795 lots.
Similarly, guar seed contracts for February delivery shed Rs 58, or 1.36 per cent, to Rs 4,202 per 10 quintal with an open interest of 55,465 lots.
Guar gum prices on Friday dropped by Rs 107 to Rs 7,559 per five quintal in futures market as traders offloaded their positions amid subdued demand.
Marketmen said trimming of positions by participants amid weak demand dragged down guar gum prices here.
On the National Commodity and Derivatives Exchange, guar gum contracts for January delivery slipped by Rs 107, or 1.4 per cent, to Rs 7,559 per five quintal in 19,370 lots.
Guar gum contracts for February delivery eased by Rs 101, or 1.3 per cent, to Rs 7,662 per five quintal in 51,360 lots.
Cottonseed oil cake prices on Friday fell by Rs 17 to Rs 2,150 per quintal in futures trade as participants reduced their bets amid a weak trend in spot market.
Market players said sell-off by participants at existing levels amid subdued trend in market mainly weighed on cottonseed oil cake prices.
On the National Commodity and Derivatives Exchange, cottonseed oil cake to be delivered in January fell by Rs 17, or 0.78 per cent, to Rs 2,150 per quintal with an open interest of 46,200 lots.
Cottonseed oil cake for delivery in February went down by Rs 16, or 0.75 per cent, to Rs 2,169 per quintal in 59,620 lots.
Coriander prices on Friday fell by Rs 118 to Rs 6,830 per quintal in futures trade as speculators reduced their positions amid sluggish demand in spot market.
On the Multi Commodity Exchange, coriander contracts for January fell by Rs 118, or 1.7 per cent, to Rs 6,830 per quintal in 7,750 lots.
Similarly, coriander contracts for April delivery declined by Rs 184, or 2.51 per cent, to Rs 7,160 per quintal in 8,090 lots.
Market analysts said subdued demand by speculators in spot market mainly led to decline in coriander prices.
