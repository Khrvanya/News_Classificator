Brazil's farmers see big soybean crops, record-high prices, record sales
April will see strong soybean sales too, Brazilian market analyst says.
INDIANOLA, Iowa -- The month of April is expected to be another big month for soybean sales out of Brazil.
Mike McGinnis, Successful Farming’s markets editor, visited, via Skype, with Daniele Siqueira, AgRural market analyst, about Brazil’s record-large soybean harvest, record-high soybean prices, and China’s insatiable appetite for that country’s soybeans.
