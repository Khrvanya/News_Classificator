It’s safe to say R&B singer, Jacquees , is having a great 2020. The success he has managed to pull off in a year like this is remarkable to watch. This year he has been nominated for a BET award in the “Best Male R&B/Pop Artist” category.  His singles “At the Club” and “You” went platinum, while “BED” is 2x platinum. Finally to top it all off, his debut album “4275” went gold.
