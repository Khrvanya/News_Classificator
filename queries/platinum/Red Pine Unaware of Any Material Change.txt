Red Pine Exploration Inc. wishes to confirm that the Company’s management is unaware of any material change  that would account for the recent increase in market activity.
At the request of IIROC, Red Pine Exploration Inc. (TSXV: RPX) (“Red Pine” or the “Company”), wishes to confirm that the Company’s management is unaware of any material change in the Company’s operations that would account for the recent increase in market activity.
Red Pine Exploration Inc. is a gold and base-metals exploration company headquartered in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. The Company’s common shares trade on the TSX Venture Exchange under the symbol “RPX”.
Red Pine has a 64.5% interest in the Wawa Gold Project with Citabar LP. holding the remaining 35.5% interest. Red Pine is the operating manager of the Project and is focused on expanding the existing gold resource on the property.
