Platinum Equity has acquired the distribution business of Deluxe Entertainment Services , a Los Angeles-based provider of subtitling, sound mastering, encryption and other services to the entertainment industry. As part of the deal, former Deluxe CEO Cyril Drabinsky will return to his role to lead the newly acquired unit. Drabinsky left the company several years ago to found CineVizion , which will now be merged with Deluxe's distribution business.
