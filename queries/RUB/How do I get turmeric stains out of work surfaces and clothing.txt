How do I get turmeric stains out of work surfaces and clothing?
I use turmeric a lot in cooking and spill on work surfaces and clothing. Nothing seems to remove these stains. Help!
Use WD-40 on surfaces and clothes. Leave about 30 seconds on the hard surface, then rub using a microfibre cloth. For clothes spray liberally, leave about 30 minutes, rinse then wash as normal. Also try nail polish remover on the work surfaces, but test a patch first in case of damage. Apply to a white cloth then wipe; it may take a few attempts.
