Miners not grasping magnitude of COVID-19, says McKinsey
Some miners have failed to grasp the potential impact of COVID-19 and must act now to shore up their balance sheets – even if they appear robust for the time being, Sigurd Mareels, a senior partner at consulting firm McKinsey, told Mining Journal.
In terms of the impact seen on mining operations to date, Mckinsey senior partner Sigurd Mareels said it was still “very early days”.
