Regina's city council will meet for a special meeting on Monday to discuss the regulatory framework for massage parlours in the city.
Posted: Sep 23, 2019 8:53 AM CT | Last Updated: September 23, 2019
The parlours would have to operate during certain hours, in certain parts of the city. There are about 20 body rub parlours in Regina, some of which may need to shut down completely or relocate once the bylaw is in effect. (CBC)
