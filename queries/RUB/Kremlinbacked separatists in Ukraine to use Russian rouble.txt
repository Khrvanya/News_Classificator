Plans to replace Ukriane’s hryvnia with the Russian rouble in parts of eastern Ukraine occupied by Kremlin-backed separatists are reportedly underway.
As reported by The Independent, the Council of Ministers in the self-proclaimed Luhansk People’s Republic (LPR) voted to adopt a decree that establishes the rouble as its official currency.
LPR is a break-away region in the south of the state of Luhansk, claimed by rebel groups allied to Moscow in May 2015.
