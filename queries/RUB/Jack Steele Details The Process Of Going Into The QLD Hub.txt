St Kilda midfielder Jack Steele explained the full process that awaits the Saints players who are heading to Noosa for their Hub.
Steele revealed when the Saints need to leave Melbourne and what the quarantine period will restrict them from doing.
He also explained that the entire playing group will be making the trip north, even players recovering from injury.
Steele said that players are allowed to bring surfboards & golf clubs up, but they won't be sent up for 14 days until their full quarantine is served.
The 24 year-old also spoke about his fascinating battle with Carlton star Patrick Cripps & the playing under new coach Brett Ratten.
