Taipei, June 29 (CNA) The U.S. dollar fell against the Taiwan dollar Monday, shedding NT$0.015 to close at NT$29.625.
Turnover totaled US$881 million during the trading session.
The greenback opened at the day's high of NT$29.660, and moved to a low of NT$29.460 before rebounding.
