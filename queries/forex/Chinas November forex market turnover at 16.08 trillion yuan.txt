BEIJING, Dec. 28 (Xinhua) -- China's foreign exchange (forex) market reported a turnover of 16.08 trillion yuan (about 2.29 trillion U.S. dollars) in November, official data showed.
Specifically, turnover of forex transactions between banks and their clients totaled 2.36 trillion yuan last month, while that of interbank forex transactions stood at 13.73 trillion yuan, according to the State Administration of Foreign Exchange.
