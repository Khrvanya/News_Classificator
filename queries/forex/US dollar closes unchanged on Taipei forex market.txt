Taipei, June 20 (CNA) The U.S. dollar closed unchanged at NT$29.660 against the Taiwan dollar Saturday.
Turnover totaled US$95 million during the trading session.
The greenback opened at the day's high of NT$29.670, and moved to a low of NT$29.570 before rebounding.
Taipei was the only foreign exchange market in the world to open Saturday to make up a lost trading session next week, when a prolonged weekend will fall to observe the Dragon Boat festival.
