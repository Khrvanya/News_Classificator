Taipei, June 30 (CNA) The U.S. dollar rose against the Taiwan dollar Tuesday, gaining NT$0.035 to close at NT$29.660.
Turnover totaled US$1.321 billion during the trading session.
The greenback opened at NT$29.630, and moved between NT$29.423 and NT$29.665 before the close.
