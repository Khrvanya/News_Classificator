NEW YORK, Nov. 18, 2019 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Nasdaq (Nasdaq: NDAQ) today announced that Exelon Corporation ( EXC ), will become a component of the NASDAQ-100 Index® (Nasdaq:NDX), the NASDAQ-100 Equal Weighted Index (Nasdaq:NDXE) and the NASDAQ-100 Ex-Tech Sector Index (Nasdaq:NDXX) prior to market open on Thursday, November 21, 2019. Exelon Corporation will replace Celgene Corporation ( CELG ) in the indexes.
Exelon Corporation is headquartered in Chicago, Illinois and has a market capitalization of approximately $44 billion. For more information about the company, go to http://www.exeloncorp.com/ .
