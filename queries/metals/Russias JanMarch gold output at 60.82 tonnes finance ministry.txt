MOSCOW, June 19 (Reuters) - Russia produced 60.82 tonnes of gold in January through March, up from 58.12 tonnes in the same period in 2019, the finance ministry said on Friday.
The country produced 210.78 tonnes of silver during the same period, down from 223.28 tonnes a year earlier. (Reporting by Darya Korsunskaya; writing by Polina Devitt; editing by Jason Neely)
