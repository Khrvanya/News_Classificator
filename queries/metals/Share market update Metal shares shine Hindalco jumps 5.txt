NEW DELHI: Metal shares were trading higher in Tuesday's session.
Shares of Hindalco Industries (up 5.03 per cent) , Welspun Corp (up 4.99 per cent) , Steel Authority of India (up 4.65 per cent) , National Aluminium Company (up 3.25 per cent) , Hindustan Copper (up 3.14 per cent) , Tata Steel (up 2.12 per cent) , JSW Steel (up 2.05 per cent) , Ratnamani Metals & Tubes (up 1.5 per cent) , MOIL (up 1.1 per cent) , Coal India (up 1.04 per cent) , Jindal Steel & Power (up 1.0 per cent) , APL Apollo Tubes (up 0.66 per cent) and VEDL (up 0.14 per cent) were among the top gainers.
The Nifty Metal index was trading 1.82 per cent up at 2087.15 around 02:06PM.
Benchmark NSE Nifty50 index was up 107.40 points at 10418.6, while the BSE Sensex was up 344.26 points at 35255.58.
Among the 50 stocks in the Nifty index, 46 were trading in the green, while 4 were in the red.
Shares of Vodafone Idea, BHEL, PNB, IDFC First Bank Ltd., Tata Motors, Glenmark Pharma, Bank of Baroda, SBI and Federal Bank were among the most traded shares on the NSE.
