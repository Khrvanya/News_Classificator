Metals prices have yet to join the latest leg down in the coronavirus-prompted market rout, a sign that some investors see China’s voracious appetite for industrial commodities recovering even as the epidemic hits the rest of the world.
Prices of copper, aluminum, iron ore and other resources have sold off this year, but have fared better than oil and equities, including the share prices of the miners who dig them up.
