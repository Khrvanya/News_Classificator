NEW DELHI: Sugar stocks were trading on a mixed note in Friday's afternoon session.
Indian Sucrose (up 4.76 per cent), Sir Shadi Lal Enterprises (up 4.66 per cent), Gayatri Sugars (up 4.00 per cent), Vishwaraj Sugar Industries (up 1.11 per cent), Dharani Sugars & Chemicals (up 0.89 per cent), Simbhaoli Sugars (up 0.83 per cent), Bannari Amman Sugars (up 0.77 per cent), Ponni Sugars (Erode) (up 0.66 per cent) and Ugar Sugar Works (up 0.54 per cent) were among the top gainers.
Kesar Enterprise (down 6.25 per cent), Magadh Sugar (down 4.29 per cent), Avadh Sugar (down 3.21 per cent), Mawana Sugars (down 2.85 per cent), Dhampur Sugar Mills (down 2.70 per cent), Rana Sugars (down 2.50 per cent), Piccadily Agro Industries (down 2.09 per cent), Rajshree Sugars & Chemicals (down 1.76 per cent) and KCP Sugar & Industries (down 1.72 per cent) were among the top losers.
The NSE Nifty 50 index was trading 12.70 points up at 12272.4, while the 30 shares BSE Sensex was down 0.80 points at 41673.12 at around 03:06 pm.
Yes Bank (up 3.71 per cent), State Bank of India (up 2.88 per cent), Tata Steel (up 2.85 per cent), Hero MotoCorp (up 1.37 per cent) and Bharti Airtel (up 1.34 per cent) were among the top gainers in the Sensex pack.
On the other hand, Vedanta (down 4.02 per cent), Kotak Mahindra Bank (down 2.08 per cent), Tata Motors (down 1.67 per cent), ITC (down 1.33 per cent), Mahindra & Mahindra (down 1.22 per cent) and Reliance Industries (down 0.5 per cent) were trading in the red.
