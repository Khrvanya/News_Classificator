Lemon Tart Lemon Tart - sweet paste Yield/batch: 2 Blitz flour and butter in a food processor until the texture resembles breadcrumbs. Add salt and sugar, blitz 20 seconds. Add the egg and blitz for 60 seconds. Tip onto bench and rub with the heel of hand to form a paste. Split into two, flatten and wrap in cling film. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes. Add lemon juice and zest. Store in a 10-litre plastic bucket. Pass through fine chinois before use. Remove all froth and foam before pouring into blind baked tart shell.
