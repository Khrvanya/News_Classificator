NEW DELHI: Most of the sugar stocks were in the green in Tuesday's morning trade.
Shares of Triveni Engineering & Industries (up 1.54 per cent), Indian Sucrose (up 1.32 per cent), Dhampur Sugar Mills (up 1.26 per cent), Uttam Sugar Mills (up 1.19 per cent) and EID Parry (up 1.14 per cent) were the top gainers.
Magadh Sugar (up 1.08 per cent), Dalmia Bharat Sugar & Industries (up 0.84 per cent), Dwarikesh Sugar Industries (up 0.84 per cent) and Shree Renuka Sugars (up 0.78 per cent) too were trading with gains.
While Rana Sugars (down 5.00 per cent), Rajshree Sugars & Chemicals (down 3.41 per cent), Balrampur Chini Mills (down 2.00 per cent), Vishwaraj Sugar Industries (down 1.08 per cent) and Sakthi Sugars (down 0.82 per cent) were among the top losers.
The NSE Nifty50 index was trading 121.20 points up at 12,152.7, while the 30share BSE Sensex was up 401.28 points at 41,380.9 at around 09:39 am.
Tata Steel (up 2.56 per cent), UltraTech Cement (up 1.91 per cent), Axis Bank (up 1.84 per cent), ITC (up 1.78 per cent) and IndusInd Bank (up 1.71 per cent) were among the top gainers in the Nifty pack.
On the other hand, Tata Consultancy (down 0.06 per cent) was alone in the red.
