NEW DELHI: Shares of sugar firms were in the green in Friday's morning trade.
Shares of Vishwaraj Sugar Industries (up 10.09 per cent), Rana Sugars (up 4.78 per cent), Indian Sucrose (up 4.72 per cent), Dwarikesh Sugar Industries (up 3.62 per cent), Bannari Amman Sugars (up 3.25 per cent), DCM Shriram Industries (up 3.21 per cent) and Ugar Sugar Works (up 2.95 per cent) were among the top gainers.
While Ponni Sugars (Erode) (down 5.49 per cent), Gayatri Sugars (down 4.00 per cent) and Piccadily Sugar & Allied Inds (down 3.80 per cent) were among the top losers.
The NSE Nifty50 index was trading 86.55 points up at 12,058.35, while the 30share BSE Sensex was up 335.15 points at 40,916.86 at around 10:27 am.
Tata Motors DVR (up 5.04 per cent), Tata Motors (up 4.99 per cent), VEDL (up 4.03 per cent), Yes Bank (up 3.09 per cent) and Tata Steel (up 3.03 per cent) were among the top gainers in the Nifty pack.
On the other hand, Bharti Airtel (down 1.38 per cent), Kotak Mahindra Bank (down 0.89 per cent), Bajaj Auto (down 0.42 per cent), Asian Paints (down 0.38 per cent) and Tech Mahindra (down 0.32 per cent) were trading in the red.
