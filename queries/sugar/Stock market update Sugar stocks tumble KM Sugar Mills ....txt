NEW DELHI: Most of the sugar stocks were in the red in Wednesday's morning trade.
Shares of KM Sugar Mills (down 5.00 per cent), Ponni Sugars (Erode) (down 4.82 per cent), Indian Sucrose (down 4.64 per cent), Uttam Sugar Mills (down 2.43 per cent), Dalmia Bharat Sugar & Industries (down 2.22 per cent) and Ugar Sugar Works (down 2.10 per cent) were among the top losers.
While Sakthi Sugars (up 7.25 per cent), Bannari Amman Sugars (up 4.58 per cent) and Simbhaoli Sugars (up 4.15 per cent) were among the top gainers.
The NSE Nifty50 index was trading 68.70 points down at 11,984.25, while the 30share BSE Sensex was down 206.85 points at 40,662.62 at around 09:38 am.
Tata Consultancy (up 1.08 per cent), Tech Mahindra (up 0.78 per cent), Bajaj Auto (up 0.37 per cent) and Infosys (up 0.06 per cent) were among the top gainers in the Nifty pack.
On the other hand, Larsen & Toubro (down 1.62 per cent), Kotak Mahindra Bank (down 1.41 per cent), HDFC Bank (down 1.23 per cent), Axis Bank (down 1.2 per cent) and State Bank of India (down 1.18 per cent) were trading in the red.
