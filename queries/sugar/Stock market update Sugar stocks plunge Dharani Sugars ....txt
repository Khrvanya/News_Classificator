NEW DELHI: Most of the sugar stocks were trading in the red in Wednesday's afternoon session.
Shares of Dharani Sugars (down 8.49 per cent), Sakthi Sugars (down 6.98 per cent), Indian Sucrose (down 4.90 per cent), Shree Renuka Sugars (down 3.91 per cent) and Balrampur Chini Mills (down 3.67 per cent) were among the top losers.
Simbhaoli Sugars (down 3.60 per cent), Rajshree Sugars & Chemicals (down 3.50 per cent), Kesar Enterprise (down 2.76 per cent) and Dalmia Bharat Sugar & Industries (down 2.67 per cent) too were trading with losses.
While Ponni Sugars (Erode) (up 4.21 per cent) and Piccadily Agro Industries (up 3.40 per cent) were among the top gainers.
The NSE Nifty50 index was trading 41.15 points down at 11,262.15, while the 30share BSE Sensex was down 161.65 points at 38,462.05 at around 12:35 pm.
Sun Pharmaceutical Industries (up 3.92 per cent), Asian Paints (up 2.73 per cent), Power Grid Corporation of India (up 1.87 per cent), Infosys (up 1.31 per cent) and Tech Mahindra (up 1.02 per cent) were among the top gainers in the Nifty pack.
On the other hand, Tata Steel (down 4.73 per cent), IndusInd Bank (down 3.02 per cent), UltraTech Cement (down 2.06 per cent), ICICI Bank (down 1.87 per cent) and HDFC Bank (down 1.82 per cent) were trading in the red.
