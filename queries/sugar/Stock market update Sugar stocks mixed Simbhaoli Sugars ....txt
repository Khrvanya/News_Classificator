NEW DELHI: Shares of sugar producers were trading on a mixed note in Monday's morning session.
Shares of Simbhaoli Sugars (up 3.82 per cent), Dharani Sugars & Chemicals (up 3.38 per cent), Thiru Arooran Sugars (up 2.50 per cent), Rana Sugars (up 2.27 per cent) and Kesar Enterprise (up 2.21 per cent) rose up to 4 per cent.
Dhampur Sugar Mills (down 2.44 per cent), EID Parry (down 2.17 per cent), Kothari Sugars & Chemicals (down 1.00 per cent), Mawana Sugars (down 0.86 per cent) and Uttam Sugar Mills (down 0.82 per cent) were among the top losers.
The NSE Nifty50 index was trading 4.95 points up at 11,179.7, the 30-share BSE Sensex was up 60.31 points at 37,733.62 at around 09:40 am.
YES Bank (up 2.85 per cent), Britannia Industries (up 1.91 per cent), Vedanta (up 1.15 per cent), Bharti Airtel (up 1.03 per cent), Hindalco Industries (up 0.80 per cent) were among the top gainers in the Nifty pack.
On the other hand, Zee Entertainment Enterprises (down 8.51 per cent), Indiabulls Housing Finance (down 8.32 per cent), Bharat Petroleum Corporation (down 3.21 per cent), IndusInd Bank (down 2.67 per cent), Sun Pharmaceutical Industries (down 1.42 per cent) were trading in the red.
