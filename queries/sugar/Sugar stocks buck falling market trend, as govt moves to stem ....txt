Sugar stocks buck falling market trend, as govt moves to stem price fall
Gain on a price rise following government's release quota, doubling import duty and a buzz of scrapping export duty

Sugar stocks bucked the broader downward trend in the benchmark Sensex and Nifty on Friday, following the government's decision to impose stock holding limits to check excess release of sugar into the market and arrest a fall in its price.
