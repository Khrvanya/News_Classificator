NEW DELHI: Most of the sugar stocks were in the green in Monday's trade.
Shares of KM Sugar Mills (up 4.93 per cent), EID Parry (up 4.70 per cent), Piccadily Agro Industries (up 4.41 per cent), DCM Shriram Industries (up 4.26 per cent) and Triveni Engineering & Industries (up 3.51 per cent) were among the top gainers.
While Piccadily Sugar & Allied Inds (down 9.21 per cent), Rajshree Sugars & Chemicals (down 5.69 per cent), Simbhaoli Sugars (down 5.00 per cent), Rana Sugars (down 4.90 per cent) and Shree Renuka Sugars (down 2.41 per cent) were among the top losers.
The NSE Nifty50 index was trading 177.90 points up at 11,379.65, while the 30share BSE Sensex was up 625.01 points at 38,922.3 at around 11:50 am.
HCL Technologies (up 4.33 per cent), ICICI Bank (up 4.23 per cent), Infosys (up 4.08 per cent), Nestle India (up 3.11 per cent) and Power Grid Corporation of India (up 2.89 per cent) were among the top gainers.
On the other hand, Bajaj Auto (down 3.51 per cent), Kotak Mahindra Bank (down 1.18 per cent) and Hero MotoCorp (down 0.2 per cent) were trading in the red.
