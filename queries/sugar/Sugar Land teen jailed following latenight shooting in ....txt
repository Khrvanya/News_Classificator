A Sugar Land teen is in jail on second-degree felony charges of aggravated assault with a deadly weapon following a late night shooting in Richmond. According to Fort Bend County Sheriff Department officials, deputies were dispatched shortly after 1 am. to a residence in the 1900 block of US 90A Eastbound to investigate reports a person was shot. FBCSD officials confirmed first-responders transported one victim to the Houston Medical Center for treatment of non-life threatening injuries. Following an investigation at the scene, Mahmood Ayaan Rehmatullah, 19, was arrested on second-degree felony charges of Aggravated Assault with a Deadly Weapon.
“This kind of reckless behavior will not be tolerated,” Sheriff Troy Nehls said in a media release.
Rehmatullah is currently being held at the Fort Bend County Jail on a $20,000 bond. According to Fort Bend County criminal court records, Rehmatullah is also facing misdemeanor charges of Family Violence Assault filed in connection with an arrest in April 2019. A fort Bend County Grand Jury is expected to review the felony assault charges and consider an indictment in the coming weeks.
