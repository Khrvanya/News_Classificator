NEW DELHI: Most of the sugar stocks were in the red in Tuesday's afternoon trade.
Shares of Bajaj Hindusthan (down 9.29 per cent), KM Sugar Mills (down 6.98 per cent), Shree Renuka Sugars (down 5.35 per cent), Indian Sucrose (down 5.00 per cent) and Simbhaoli Sugars (down 5.00 per cent) were among the top losers.
While Dharani Sugars & Chemicals (up 9.98 per cent), Magadh Sugar (up 1.15 per cent) and Mawana Sugars (up 1.05 per cent) were among the top gainers.
The NSE Nifty50 index was trading 33.95 points up at 11,166.7, while the 30share BSE Sensex was up 43.62 points at 38,187.64 at around 11:42 am.
Sun Pharmaceutical Industries (up 3.59 per cent), Oil & Natural Gas Corporation (up 2.36 per cent), Tata Steel (up 2.24 per cent), Hero MotoCorp (up 1.85 per cent) and HCL Technologies (up 1.69 per cent) were among the top gainers in the Nifty pack.
On the other hand, Bajaj Auto (down 5.01 per cent), HDFC Bank (down 1.08 per cent), Titan Company (down 0.9 per cent), Asian Paints (down 0.81 per cent) and Kotak Mahindra Bank (down 0.58 per cent) were trading in the red.
