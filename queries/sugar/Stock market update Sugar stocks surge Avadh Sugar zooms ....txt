NEW DELHI: Most of the sugar stocks were trading with gains in Wednesday's morning session.
Shares of Avadh Sugar (up 5.92 per cent), Dalmia Bharat Sugar & Industries (up 5.78 per cent), Magadh Sugar (up 5.16 per cent), Uttam Sugar Mills (up 5.03 per cent) and Dwarikesh Sugar Industries (up 4.34 per cent) were the top gainers.
Mawana Sugars (up 3.27 per cent), Shree Renuka Sugars (up 3.23 per cent), Triveni Engineering & Industries (up 3.19 per cent) and Dhampur Sugar Mills (up 3.08 per cent) too were in the green.
Whie Indian Sucrose (down 4.89 per cent), Rana Sugars (down 4.84 per cent), Piccadily Agro Industries (down 4.34 per cent) and Vishwaraj Sugar Industries (down 0.26 per cent) were among the top losers.
The NSE Nifty50 index was trading 91.15 points up at 12,199.05, while the 30share BSE Sensex was up 340.12 points at 41,556.26 at around 09:53 am.
Hindustan Unilever (up 4.14 per cent), Nestle India (up 1.85 per cent), HDFC (up 1.54 per cent), NTPC (up 1.16 per cent) amd Axis Bank (up 1.15 per cent) were among the top gainers in the Nifty pack.
On the other hand, IndusInd Bank (down 1.95 per cent) and ITC (down 0.33 per cent) were trading in the red.
