NEW DELHI: Sugar stocks were trading on a positive note in Tuesday's afternoon session.
Shares of K.M.Sugar Mills (up 10.94 per cent), KCP Sugar & Industries (up 7.49 per cent), Ugar Sugar Works (up 5.93 per cent), Mawana Sugars (up 5.74 per cent), Magadh Sugar (up 5.31 per cent), Dwarikesh Sugar Industries (up 4.81 per cent), Rana Sugars (up 4.72 per cent), Sakthi Sugars (up 4.64 per cent) and Rajshree Sugars & Chemicals (up 4.44 per cent) were among the top gainers.
While Riga Sugar Company (down 4.21 per cent), Parvati Sweetners (down 3.82 per cent), Bannari Amman Sugars (down 1.73 per cent) and Dharani Sugars & Chemicals (down 1.16 per cent) were among the top losers.
The NSE Nifty-50 index was trading 27.40 points up at 12020.45, while the 30 shares BSE Sensex was up 95.04 points at 40771.67 at around 01:22 pm.
