Is this like the war on drugs?
It’s quite different, not least because this war might actually be winnable. New research1 has shown that in Chile—a nation beset by obesity—sales of sugary drinks dropped by nearly a quarter in two years after the country introduced a “uniquely comprehensive” set of tough new obesity prevention policies in 2016.
