NEW DELHI: Sugar stocks were trading in the positive zone in Friday's afternoon session.
Shares of K.M.Sugar Mills (up 8.23 per cent), Dalmia Bharat Sugar & Industries (up 7.42 per cent), Sir Shadi Lal Enterprises (up 4.89 per cent), Dwarikesh Sugar Industries (up 4.76 per cent), Thiru Arooran Sugars (up 4.68 per cent), Balrampur Chini Mills (up 4.48 per cent), DCM Shriram Industries (up 4.37 per cent), Rajshree Sugars & Chemicals (up 4.25 per cent) and Sakthi Sugars (up 3.71 per cent) were among the top gainers.
While Gayatri Sugars (down 3.70 per cent), Rana Sugars (down 2.26 per cent), Piccadily Sugar & Allied Industries (down 1.92 per cent), Magadh Sugar (down 1.57 per cent), Simbhaoli Sugars (down 0.81 per cent), Ugar Sugar Works (down 0.15 per cent) and Piccadily Agro Industries (down 0.14 per cent) were among the top losers.
The NSE Nifty 50 index was trading 91.50 points up at 12218.05, while the 30 shares BSE Sensex was up 321.56 points at 41485.32 at around 02:39 pm.
