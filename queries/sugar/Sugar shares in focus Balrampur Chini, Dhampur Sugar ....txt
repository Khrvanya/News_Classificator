Shares of sugar companies were in focus in Wednesday's session and rallied by up to 8 per cent on the BSE on expectation of no significant upward revision in sugar production. Balrampur Chini Mills, Avadh Sugar & Energy, Dhampur Sugar Mills, Dalmia Bharat Sugar and Industries, Uttam Sugar Mills and Dwarikesh Sugar Industries were up 5 per cent to 8 per cent in intra-day trade today.
