NEW DELHI: Most of the sugar stocks were trading with losses in Tuesday's morning session.
Shares of Triveni Engineering & Industries (down 3.57 per cent), KM Sugar Mills (down 2.39 per cent), Dalmia Bharat Sugar & Industries (down 1.99 per cent), DCM Shriram Industries (down 1.81 per cent) and Balrampur Chini Mills (down 1.77 per cent) were among the top losers.
Dhampur Sugar Mills (down 1.63 per cent), Uttam Sugar Mills (down 1.05 per cent) and Mawana Sugars (down 1.01 per cent) too were in the red.
While Piccadily Agro Industries (up 6.12 per cent), Sir Shadi Lal Enterprises (up 4.92 per cent), Rana Sugars (up 1.10 per cent) and Magadh Sugar (up 0.36 per cent) were among the top gainers.
The NSE Nifty50 index was trading 28.25 points up at 11,857.65, while the 30share BSE Sensex was up 114.18 points at 40,477.41 at around 10:05 am.
NTPC (up 2.28 per cent), Oil & Natural Gas Corporation (up 1.99 per cent), Hindustan Unilever (up 1.76 per cent), Mahindra & Mahindra (up 1.06 per cent) and Tata Steel (up 1.02 per cent) were among the top gainers in the Nifty pack.
On the other hand, HCL Technologies (down 1.87 per cent), Sun Pharmaceutical Industries (down 1.86 per cent), Titan Company Ltd (down 1.2 per cent), Tech Mahindra (down 1.15 per cent) and HDFC Bank (down 0.73 per cent) were trading in the red.
