NEW DLEHI: Shares of sugar producers were trading with gains in Monday's morning trade.
Shares of Rajshree Sugars & Chemicals (up 5.00 per cent), Gayatri Sugars (up 4.73 per cent), Sir Shadi Lal Enterprises (up 4.67 per cent), Piccadily Sugar & Allied Industries (up 4.67 per cent) and DCM Shriram Industries (up 4.30 per cent) gained up to 5 per cent.
Mawana Sugars (up 2.31 per cent), Dhampur Sugar Mills (up 2.26 per cent) and Triveni Engineering & Industries (up 1.71 per cent) too were trading in the positive zone.
While share of Simbhaoli Sugars (down 8.06 per cent), Dharani Sugars & Chemicals (down 4.92 per cent), Magadh Sugar (down 2.44 per cent) and Bajaj Hindusthan Sugar (down 2.32 per cent) were among the top losers.
The NSE Nifty50 index was trading 54.85 points up at 11,001.05, while the 30-share BSE Sensex was up 191.98 points at 37,173.75 at around 10:43 am.
UPL (up 2.70 per cent), ICICI Bank (up 2.07 per cent), YES Bank (up 1.74 per cent), Hindustan Unilever (up 1.68 per cent), Larsen & Toubro (up 1.26 per cent), Bajaj Finance (up 1.16 per cent) were among the top gainers in the Nifty pack.
On the other hand, HCL Technologies (down 1.06 per cent), Tech Mahindra (down 0.99 per cent), Tata Motors (down 0.91 per cent), Eicher Motors (down 0.84 per cent), Bajaj Auto (down 0.75 per cent) were trading in the red.
