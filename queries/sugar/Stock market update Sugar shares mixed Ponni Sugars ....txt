NEW DELHI: Most of the sugar stocks were trading on a mixed note in Wednesday's session.
Shares of Piccadily Sugar (up 9.75 per cent), Bajaj Hindusthan (up 6.87 per cent), Piccadily Agro Industries (up 6.40 per cent), Sakthi Sugars (up 3.47 per cent) and Shree Renuka Sugars (up 2.18 per cent) were among the top gainers.
While Ponni Sugars (Erode) (down 12.57 per cent), Kesar Enterprise (down 8.00 per cent), Dwarikesh Sugar Industries (down 6.18 per cent), Indian Sucrose (down 4.99 per cent) and Triveni Engineering & Industries (down 3.74 per cent) were among the top losers.
The NSE Nifty50 index was trading 28.85 points down at 10,422.6, while the 30share BSE Sensex was down 39.66 points at 35,595.29 at around 11:33 am.
Hero MotoCorp (up 3.72 per cent), Reliance Industries (up 3.58 per cent), ICICI Bank (up 1.2 per cent), Bajaj Auto (up 1.01 per cent) and Bharti Airtel (up 0.7 per cent) were among the top gainers in the Nifty pack.
On the other hand, Tata Steel (down 4.41 per cent), Infosys (down 2.95 per cent), Sun Pharmaceutical Industries (down 2.7 per cent), UltraTech Cement (down 2.21 per cent) and IndusInd Bank (down 2.01 per cent) were trading in the red.
