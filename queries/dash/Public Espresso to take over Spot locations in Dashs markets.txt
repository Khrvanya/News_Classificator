BUFFALO, N.Y. — Coffee drinkers take note: there is a slight change brewing at Dash’s Markets.
Beginning early next year, Public Espresso + Coffee will be taking over the Spot Coffee operations inside the area’s Dash’s supermarkets.
Public Espresso + Coffee is growing its presence after launching from a flagship location is in the Hotel@Lafayette. Dash’s has four locations in Buffalo, Tonawanda, Clarence and Amherst. You can read more on Buffalo Business First's website .
