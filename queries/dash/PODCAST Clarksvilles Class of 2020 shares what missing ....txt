CLARKSVILLE, Tenn. (CLARKSVILLENOW) – The Class of 2020 has been through a lot. Your senior year comes with mixed emotions, but this year has been unlike any other.
Clarksville’s Conversation sat down with several seniors in the community to hear their perspective on these unprecedented times. Missed proms, sports seasons cut short, and the cancellation of graduation has hit these young adults hard.
But through it all they remain resilient, positive, and provide listeners with a unique outlook on COVID-19.
