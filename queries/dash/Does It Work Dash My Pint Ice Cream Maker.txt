The Dash My Pint Ice Cream Maker promises you can make tasty ice cream at home.
Published: 11:05 PM CST January 22, 2020
Updated: 11:05 PM CST January 22, 2020
AUSTIN, Texas — Every day is a good day for ice cream. Soft, luscious, flavorful – who can resist it?
The Dash My Pint Ice Cream Maker promises you can make the tasty treat at home and make "sundae" every day!
The product comes with a plastic paddle, mixing spoon, motor unit, cooling container and cooling core. Note: You have to put the core in the freezer for 24 hours before you can use the ice cream maker.
Directions say you can make classic vanilla ice cream, frozen yogurt and even raspberry sorbet!
