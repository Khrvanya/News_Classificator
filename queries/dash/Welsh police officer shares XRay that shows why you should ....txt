This horrifying X-ray shows what can happen if you have your feet on a dashboard during a car crash.
The warning is from Dyfed Powys police officer Ian Price who is urging people to stop doing it.
He says: "Here is an X-ray of horrific injuries sustained to the front seat passenger who had their feet on the dashboard at the time of a collision.
There is currently no law that stops a passenger from putting their feet on the dashboard while the vehicle is moving.
But a police officer could accuse you of impeding the operation of a motor vehicle because the passenger would be blocking the passenger side mirror.
