A police officer was injured when a semi truck rolled over onto a patrol car in an incident caught on camera in Smyrna, Georgia, last week.
The incident happened at the intersection of Windy Hill and Atlanta Roads around 10:30 a.m. June 25, 2020.
The Smyrna and Cobb County Fire Departments were on the scene working to free the officer from the car.
The officer was extracted around 11:00 a.m. and taken to the Kennestone Hospital with non-life-threatening injuries.
