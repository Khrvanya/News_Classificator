Chicago White Sox Prospects Nick Madrigal, Luis Robert Set to Take Field in Future's Game
Two of the Chicago White Sox top prospects will take the field in Cleveland on Sunday evening as part of the MLB All-Star Futures Game.
Infielder Nick Madrigal, who was taken with the fourth overall pick in the 2018 MLB Draft, will play second base and bat second for the American League squad in the game, which will start at 6 p.m. Sunday night.
Madrigal, currently the fourth-rated prospect in the White Sox system according to MLB Pipeline, has a combined two home runs and 38 RBI this season in Class-A and Double-A, and has stolen 28 bases with the Winston-Salem Dash and the Birmingham Barons.
