Britain’s Premier League is finally returning next week. It could be a bad omen for Wall Street.
American Airlines is among the top five most traded stocks at U.K. financial spread-betting firm IG, the company said in a blog post Wednesday. It is the latest sign of the “dash for trash” that has led individual investors to make wagers on the riskiest stocks this month, including bankrupt rental-car company Hertz.
