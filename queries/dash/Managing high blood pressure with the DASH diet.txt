(WKOW) -- February is Heart Health Awareness month, a time for many people to reflect on how they take care of their hearts.
According to the American Heart Association , nearly half of American adults have high blood pressure.
Becky Kerkenbush, a dietitian with the Wisconsin Academy of Nutrition and Dietetics, explains how adults with high blood pressure, can manage it with the DASH diet.
