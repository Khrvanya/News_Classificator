Dash's Markets will set aside certain shopping hours for medical professionals during the coronavirus pandemic.
Doctors, nurses and all first responders will be given priority at Dash's Market stores from 6 a.m. to 7 a.m. on Tuesdays, Thursdays and Saturdays
The special hours start Saturday.
"Based on the positive response to our special hours for seniors and expectant mothers, we felt compelled to assist the healthcare workers and first responders with easier access for their grocery shopping needs" Joe Dash, Dash's CEO, said in a statement.
Dash's Market offers exclusive shopping hours for seniors and expectant moms from 6 a.m. to 7 a.m. Monday, Wednesday and Friday.
