Gold futures fell on Friday to post a loss of about 3% for the week. Optimism over a drug that shows potential to treat COVID-19 and the release of a plan to gradually reopen the U.S. economy combined to dull demand for the haven metal. June gold gcm20 lost $32.90, or 1.9%, to settle at $1,698.80 an ounce. Prices lost 3.1% for the week, according to FactSet data.
