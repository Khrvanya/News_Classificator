Gold futures fall sharply for the session, climb for the month
Gold futures fell sharply on Tuesday , but scored a gain for month against a backdrop of ongoing worries about the spread of the coronavirus and overall losses in the stock market. June gold gcm20 fell $46.60, or 2.8%, to settle at $1,596.60 an ounce. The contract, which is now the most active, was up 1.6% from its finish at $1,571.80 on Feb. 28, according to FactSet data.
