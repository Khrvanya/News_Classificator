Gold futures fall for the session, edge higher for the week
Gold futures settled with a loss on Friday , pressured as U.S. benchmark stock indexes climbed despite data showing a loss of more than 20 million U.S. jobs in April. June gold gcm20 fell $11.90, or 0.7%, to settle at $1,713.90 an ounce. For the week, prices for the most-active contract were still up by 0.8%, according to FactSet data.
