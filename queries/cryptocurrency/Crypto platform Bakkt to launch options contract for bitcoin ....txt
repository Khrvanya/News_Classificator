(Reuters) - Cryptocurrency platform Bakkt Trust Co LLC said on Thursday it will launch a regulated options contract for bitcoin futures on Dec. 9.
The bitcoin options contract will be based on the benchmark Bakkt Monthly Bitcoin Futures, the company said in a blog post. ( bit.ly/2pSK7lr )
Bakkt is an affiliate of Intercontinental Exchange, which also owns the New York Stock Exchange.
