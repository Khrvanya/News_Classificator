BURLINGTON, Vt. (WCAX) The University of Vermont's College of Agriculture and Life Sciences has a new dean.
Leslie Parise comes to Vermont from the University of North Carolina, where she worked as a professor before spending the last decade as the chair of the department of biochemistry and biophysics.
Parise spoke with our Darren Perron about her plans at UVM and how the school can help struggling Vermont dairy farmers. Watch the video for the full interview.
