The value of Polish trade on the internet in 2020 may exceed PLN 100 bln (EUR 22.4 bln), Finance Minister Tadeusz Kościński was quoted on Twitter as saying by the ministry.
"Last year, the value of Polish e-commerce exceeded PLN 50 bln (EUR 11.2 bln). This year there is a good chance that it will exceed PLN 100 billion (EUR 22.4 bln). According to forecasts, in the next five years, trade over the Internet will grow by 20 percent year on year," said Kościński.
