The Polish banking sector posted a net profit of PLN 3.38 billion (EUR 0.75 billion) in January-May 2020, a 42.2 percent decrease year on year, the National Bank of Poland (NBP) said in a Friday report.
The sector's operational income reached PLN 28.6 billion (EUR 6.4 billion) in January-May, declining by 2.9 percent year on year. Overheads stood at PLN 15.5 billion (EUR 3.4 billion), falling by 0.3 percent from the previous year.
Impairments soared by 43.8 percent year on year in the first five months of the year, reaching PLN 5.14 bln (EUR 1.14 billion).
