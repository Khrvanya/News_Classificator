The RHONJ mom looks totally different with this bold hairstyle.
Teresa Giudice is no stranger to trying different hair colors, from dark brown waves to honey blonde highlights . But The Real Housewives of New Jersey mom may have just rocked her boldest look yet, donning a short, platinum blonde locks in a recent Instagram photo.
On June 19, Teresa took to Instagram to share a look at herself looking trendy and glamorous in a uber-blonde hairstyle that just barely grazes her shoulder — a far cry from her usual, waist-length extensions. “I Do A Thing Called What I Want,” the mom of four wrote alongside the photo
So is Teresa really leaving her brunette hair behind? Not quite. The platinum blonde bob was just one of several colorful wigs she took for a spin during a recent visit to her hairstylist.
