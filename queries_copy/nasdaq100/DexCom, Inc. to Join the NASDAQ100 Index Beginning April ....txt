NEW YORK, April  10, 2020  (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Nasdaq (Nasdaq: NDAQ) today announced that DexCom, Inc. (Nasdaq: DXCM), will become a component of the NASDAQ-100 Index® (Nasdaq: NDX), the NASDAQ-100 Equal Weighted Index (Nasdaq: NDXE) and the NASDAQ-100 Ex-Tech Sector Index (Nasdaq: NDXX) prior to market open on Monday, April 20, 2020. DexCom, Inc. will replace American Airlines Group, Inc. (Nasdaq: AAL) in the indexes.
DexCom, Inc. is headquartered in San Diego, California and has a market capitalization of approximately $25.2 billion. For more information about the company, go to https://www.dexcom.com /.
