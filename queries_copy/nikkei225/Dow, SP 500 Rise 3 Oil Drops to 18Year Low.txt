U.S. stocks rose and oil prices dropped to an 18-year low Monday after government officials signaled that measures to contain the coronavirus pandemic are likely to remain in place for an extended time.
The Dow Jones Industrial Average and S&P 500 have rallied more than 15% since bottoming a week ago but remain down more than 20% from their February records as the pandemic has closed businesses, reduced air travel and pushed millions of people to work from home.
