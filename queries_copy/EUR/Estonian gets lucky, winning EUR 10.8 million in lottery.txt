A person in Estonia, has won a 10.8-million-euro jackpot in the international Viking Lotto lottery. The sum has set an Estonian record in the area, Estonian public broadcaster ERR reports .
The lucky draw took place on Wednesday, July 1, in the Viking Lotto lottery, which is jointly organised by Baltic and Nordic national lottery companies.
The lottery numbers on the winning ticket, which was bought online, were: 11, 18, 20, 30, 45, 46 and the extra number was 2.
This win marks the largest lotto result in Estonian history, as the previous Estonian record was EUR 1.155 million, which was unbeaten from 2016, ERR reports.
