CoinDCX, a cryptocurrency and bitcoin exchange company , announced today the launch of DCXfutures, it will maximize the potential of traders with leverage up to 15x.
A Bitcoin futures contract is available to trade on DCXfutures along with futures trading on 8 leading altcoins; Ether (ETH), XRP (XRP), Bitcoin Cash (BCH), Litecoin (LTC), Eos (EOS), Cardano (ADA), and Tron (TRX). Users can also trade perpetual future contracts with Bitcoin and Ethereum.
In March 2019, CoinDCX completed a seed round of financing led by Bain Capital Ventures.
