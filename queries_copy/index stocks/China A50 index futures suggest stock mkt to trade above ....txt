Jul 01, 2020 (China Knowledge) - BJT 09:01 - FTSE China A50 opens at 13,718. The index remains relatively unchanged from the previous close. The index is 15 points higher or up 0.11% than the previous close.
Yesterday the Shanghai Composite Index closed at 2,984.67, up 0.78%, and Shenzhen Component Index finished at 11,992.35, up 2.04%, the Hang Seng Index closed at 24,427.19, up 0.52%. The trading volume for three exchanges recorded USD 122.6 bln, up 2.48% from the previous trading day.
The markets turned up 3,134 gainers, 559 losers, and 142 remained unchanged. There were 101 triggered the limit up 10% ‘circuit breakers’ and 19 hit the limit down 10% cap.
Dow Jones closed at 25,812.88, and the NASDAQ closed at 10,058.77 last night.
