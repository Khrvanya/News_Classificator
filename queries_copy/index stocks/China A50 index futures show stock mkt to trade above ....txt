Jun 23, 2020 (China Knowledge) - BJT 09:01 - FTSE China A50 opens at 13,808. The index takes off on the positive ground. The index is 55 points higher or up 0.40% than the previous close.
Yesterday the Shanghai Composite Index closed at 2,965.27, down 0.08%, and Shenzhen Component Index finished at 11,702.44, up 0.29%, the Hang Seng Index closed at 24,511.34, down 0.54%. The trading volume for three exchanges recorded USD 133.7 bln, up 5.67% from the previous trading day.
The markets turned up 1,746 gainers, 1,933 losers, and 159 remained unchanged. There were 60 triggered the limit up 10% ‘circuit breakers’ and 27 hit the limit down 10% cap.
Dow Jones closed at 26,024.96, and the NASDAQ closed at 10,056.47 last night.
