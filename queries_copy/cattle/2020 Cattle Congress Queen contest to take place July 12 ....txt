WATERLOO, Iowa (KWWL) - The 2020 Cattle Congress Queen contest is scheduled to take place Sunday, July 12, and applications are now available.
The contest is open to young women ages 16 to 20 who live in Black Hawk County or surrounding counties.  The young woman named Cattle Congress Queen will be able to participate in the 2021 Iowa State Fair Queen Contest.
If interested, call the Cattle Congress office at 319-234-7515 or e-mail the office at info@nationalcattlecongress.com for an application.
The contest will take place at the Electric Park Ballroom.
