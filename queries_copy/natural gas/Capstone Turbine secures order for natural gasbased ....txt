|About: Capstone Turbine Corporation (CPST) |By: Vandana Singh , SA News Editor
Capstone Turbine ( CPST +12.3%) secures an order for a C600 Signature Series microturbine (C600S) fueled by natural gas, from the Russian Ministry of Sports, to be installed at the Churapcha Institute of Physical Culture and Sports.
DV Energy, Capstone's distributor in the Far Eastern Federal District in Russia, secures the order for the 600-kilowatt microturbine system, which is expected to be commissioned in Fall 2020.
The five-bay microturbine enclosure will be installed to accommodate future expansion up to one megawatt.
