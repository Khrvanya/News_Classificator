New Delhi: Coriander prices on Wednesday increased by Rs 38 to Rs 6,421 per quintal in futures trade on spot demand and restricted supplies from producing belts.
On the National Commodity and Derivatives Exchange, coriander for April delivery shot up by Rs 38, or 0.6 per cent, to Rs 6,421 per quintal with an open interest of 1,470 lots.
Coriander for May delivery traded higher by Rs 10, or 0.16 per cent, at Rs 6,341 per quintal in 4,410 lots. A firm trend in spot market and restricted supplies from producing regions pushed up coriander prices here, market analysts said.
Guar seed prices on Wednesday edged lower by Rs 20 to Rs 3,518 per 10 quintal in futures trade as participants reduced their holdings in line with a weak spot market.
Marketmen attributed the downward trend in guar seed prices to increasing supplies from growing regions.
On the National Commodity and Derivatives Exchange, guar seed contracts for April delivery fell by Rs 20, or 0.57 per cent, to Rs 3,518 per 10 quintal with an open interest of 3,445 lots.
Similarly, guar seed contracts for May delivery shed Rs 62, or 1.73 per cent, to Rs 3,514 per 10 quintal with an open interest of 42,780 lots.
Soybean prices on Wednesday went up by 0.26 per cent to Rs 3,876 per quintal in futures market as traders created fresh positions tracking positive spot demand.
On the National Commodity and Derivatives Exchange, soybean for April delivery rose by Rs 10, or 0.26 per cent, to Rs 3,876 per quintal with an open interest of 26,300 lots.
Marketmen said raising of fresh positions by speculators on the back of higher demand mainly led to rise in soybean prices for April delivery.
However, Soybean for May delivery dropped by Rs 18, or 0.47 per cent, to Rs 3,848 per quintal with an open interest of 87,710 lots.
Guar gum prices on Wednesday slumped 2.61 per cent to Rs 5,561 per five quintal in futures market as speculators offloaded their holdings in line with weak trend at the spot market.
On the National Commodity and Derivatives Exchange, guar gum for April delivery fell by Rs 149, or 2.61 per cent, to Rs 5,561 per five quintal in 1,365 lots.
Guar gum for May delivery eased by Rs 184, or 3.18 per cent, to Rs 5,595 per five quintal in 45,055 lots.
Marketmen said slackness in demand in the spot market and increased supplies from growing regions mainly exerted pressure on guar gum prices.
Refined soya oil prices on Wednesday rose by Rs 9.4 to Rs 838.6 per 10 kg in futures trade as speculators raised their bets.
On the National Commodity and Derivatives Exchange, refined soya oil for April delivery moved up by Rs 9.4, or 1.13 per cent, to Rs 838.6 per 10 kg in 26,300 lots.
Similarly, refined soya oil contracts for May delivery edged up by Rs 10, or 1.25 per cent, to Rs 811 per 10 kg in 21,620 lots.
Analysts said widening of positions by traders mainly helped refined soya oil prices to trade higher in futures market.
