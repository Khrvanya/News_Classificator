Soybean prices on Monday fell by Rs 106 to Rs 3,940 per quintal in futures market as traders cut down their positions amid weak cues from physical markets.
On the National Commodity and Derivatives Exchange, soybean to be delivered in February contracts moved down by Rs 106, or 2.62 per cent, to Rs 3,940 per quintal with an open interest of 48,510 lots.
Soybean for delivery in March contracts also eased by Rs 124, or 3.06 per cent, to Rs 3,926 per quintal with an open interest of 1,47,120 lots.
The fall in soybean prices in futures trade was mostly due to reduction of exposure by participants, marketmen said.
Mustard seed prices on Monday dropped by Rs 25 to Rs 3,967 per quintal in futures trade as speculators cut down their positions on subdued demand.
On the National Commodity and Derivatives Exchange, mustard seed contracts for February delivery fell by Rs 25, or 0.63 per cent, to Rs 3,967 per quintal in a business turnover of 7,340 lots.
Mustard seed contracts for April delivery also declined by Rs 55, or 1.36 per cent, to Rs 3,997 per quintal in a business turnover of 17,420 lots.
Marketmen said offloading of positions by participants following a weak trend in spot market mainly led to fall in mustard seed prices.
Refined soya oil prices on Monday fell by Rs 6.2 to Rs 843.2 per 10 kg in futures trade as speculators reduced holdings amid subdued demand in spot market.
On the National Commodity and Derivatives Exchange, refined soya oil for delivery in February declined by Rs 6.2, or 0.73 per cent, to Rs 843.2 per 10 kg in 22,585 lots.
The refined soya oil contracts for March dropped by Rs 9.2, or 1.1 per cent, to Rs 829.2 per 10 kg in 54,250 lots.
Market analysts said cutting down of positions by participants against ample stocks mainly influenced refined soya oil prices.
Guar seed prices on Monday edged lower by Rs 86 to Rs 3,832 per 10 quintal in futures trade as participants reduced their holdings in line with a weak spot demand.
Marketmen attributed the downward trend in guar seed prices to increasing supplies from growing regions.
On the National Commodity and Derivatives Exchange, guar seed contracts for February delivery fell by Rs 86, or 2.19 per cent, to Rs 3,832 per 10 quintal with an open interest of 21,200 lots.
Similarly, guar seed contracts for March delivery shed Rs 92, or 2.32 per cent, to Rs 3,870 per 10 quintal with an open interest of 67,270 lots.
Guar gum prices on Monday eased by Rs 183 to Rs 6,753 per five quintal in futures market as traders reduced their positions tracking weak trend at the spot market.
Marketmen said trimming of positions by participants amid weak demand mainly dragged down guar gum prices here.
On the National Commodity and Derivatives Exchange, guar gum contracts for February delivery slipped by Rs 183, or 2.64 per cent, to Rs 6,753 per five quintal in 6,930 lots.
Guar gum contracts for March delivery eased by Rs 189, or 2.69 per cent, to Rs 6,830 per five quintal in 54,115 lots.
Cottonseed oil cake prices on Monday fell by Rs 39 to Rs 1,754 per quintal in futures trade as participants reduced their bets amid a weak trend at the spot market.
Market players said sell-off by participants at the existing levels amid subdued trend in market mainly weighed on cottonseed oil cake prices.
On the National Commodity and Derivatives Exchange, cottonseed oil cake to be delivered in February fell by Rs 39, or 2.18 per cent, to Rs 1,754 per quintal with an open interest 31,220 lots.
Cottonseed oil cake for delivery in March went down by Rs 39, or 2.16 per cent, to Rs 1,772 per quintal in 73,900 lots.
Coriander prices on Monday fell by Rs 175 to Rs 6,150 per quintal in futures trade as speculators reduced their positions amid weak demand at spot market.
On the Multi Commodity Exchange, coriander contracts for April slumped by Rs 175, or 2.77 per cent, to Rs 6,150 per quintal in 7,680 lots.
Similarly, the spice for May delivery traded lower by Rs 190, or 2.99 per cent, at Rs 6,170 per quintal in 740 lots.
Market analysts said subdued demand in spot market mainly led to decline in coriander prices here.
