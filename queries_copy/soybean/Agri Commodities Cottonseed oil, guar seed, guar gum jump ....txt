Cottonseed oil cake prices on Tuesday jumped by Rs 4 to Rs 1,757 per quintal in futures trade.
Guar gum prices on Tuesday gained Rs 25 to Rs 6,877 per five quintal in futures trade.
Cottonseed oil cake prices on Tuesday jumped by Rs 4 to Rs 1,757 per quintal in futures trade due to accumulation of positions amid upbeat trend in spot market.
On the National Commodity and Derivatives Exchange, cottonseed oil cake contracts for February delivery rose by Rs 4, or 0.23 per cent, to Rs 1,757 per quintal in 48,050 lots.
Cottonseed oil cake contracts for March gained Rs 6, or 0.34 per cent, to Rs 1,777 per quintal in 64,650 lots.
Marketmen said raising of positions by participants amid rising demand from cattle-feed makers mainly influenced cottonseed oil cake prices here.
Guar gum prices on Tuesday gained Rs 25 to Rs 6,877 per five quintal in futures trade as investors increased their holdings on spot demand.
Analysts said after tracking firm physical market trend, traders raised their bets which led to rise in guar gum prices.
On the National Commodity and Derivatives Exchange, guar gum delivery for February contracts gained 0.36 per cent, or Rs 25, to Rs 6,877 per five quintal with an open interest of 17,825 lots.
Likewise, the delivery of March contract surged by Rs 21, or 0.3 per cent, to Rs 6,960 per five quintal as open interest stood at 47,450 lots.
Guar seed prices on Tuesday rose by Rs 8 to Rs 3,866 per 10 quintal in futures trade after participants widened their holdings driven by a firm trend in spot market.
On the National Commodity and Derivatives Exchange, guar seed for February contracts rose by Rs 8, or 0.21 per cent, to Rs 3,866 per 10 quintal with an open interest of 46,110 lots.
Similarly, guar seed for March delivery gained Rs 8 to Rs 3,916 per 10 quintal with an open interest lots of 44,820.
According to marketmen, raising of bets by speculators tracking a firm trend in spot market on thin supplies from growing belts mainly led to rise in guar seed prices here.
Mustard seed prices on Tuesday rose by Rs 43 to Rs 4,125 per quintal in futures trade as speculators raised their bets amid limited supplies.
Marketmen said apart from restricted arrivals from growing belts demand from oil mills in spot market mainly lifted mustard seed prices here.
On the National Commodity and Derivatives Exchange, mustard seed for delivery in February rose by Rs 43, or 1.05 per cent, to Rs 4,125 per quintal with an open interest of 11,900 lots.
Soybean prices on Tuesday rose by Rs 24 to Rs 4,076 per quintal in futures market as traders created fresh positions on strong spot demand.
On the National Commodity and Derivatives Exchange, soybean for February delivery rose by Rs 24, or 0.59 per cent, to Rs 4,076 per quintal with an open interest of 95,420 lots.
Soybean for March delivery rose by Rs 26, or 0.64 per cent, to Rs 4,074 per quintal with an open interest of 124,190 lots.
Market players said raising of fresh positions by speculators on the back of higher demand led to rise in soybean prices.
Refined soya oil prices on Tuesday rose 0.98 per cent to Rs 845 per 10 kg in futures trade as speculators raised their bets.
On the National Commodity and Derivatives Exchange, refined soya oil for February delivery moved up by Rs 8.2, or 0.98 per cent, to Rs 845 per 10 kg in 38,245 lots.
Similarly, refined soya oil contracts for March delivery edged up by Rs 11.4, or 11.39 per cent, to Rs 832 per 10 kg in 48,015 lots.
Analysts said widening of positions by traders mainly helped refined soya oil prices to trade higher in futures market here.
