Coriander prices on Thursday fell by Rs 83 to Rs 6,961 per quintal in futures trade as speculators reduced their positions amid sluggish demand in spot market.
On the Multi Commodity Exchange, coriander contracts for January fell by Rs 83, or 1.18 per cent, to Rs 6,961 per quintal in 8,050 lots.
Similarly, coriander contracts for April delivery declined by Rs 197, or 2.61 per cent, to Rs 7,351 per quintal in 7,890 lots.
Cottonseed oil cake prices on Thursday edged up by Re 1 to Rs 2,169 per quintal in futures trade due to accumulation of positions amid upbeat trend in spot market.
On the National Commodity and Derivatives Exchange, cottonseed oil cake contracts for January delivery rose by Re 1, or 0.05 per cent, to Rs 2,169 per quintal in 48,030 lots.
Cottonseed oil cake contracts for February gained Rs 4, or 0.18 per cent, to Rs 2,190 per quintal in 56,730 lots.
Marketmen said raising of positions by participants amid rising demand from cattle-feed makers mainly influenced prices here.
Guar gum prices on Thursday dropped by Rs 17 to Rs 7,715 per five quintal in futures market as traders offloaded their positions amid subdued demand.
Marketmen said trimming of positions by participants amid weak demand dragged down guar gum prices here.
On the National Commodity and Derivatives Exchange, guar gum contracts for January delivery slipped by Rs 17, or 0.22 per cent, to Rs 7,715 per five quintal in 21,305 lots.
Guar gum contracts for February delivery eased by Rs 23, or 0.29 per cent, to Rs 7,809 per five quintal in 48,900 lots.
Guar seed prices on Thursday fell by Rs 2 to Rs 4,230 per 10 quintal in futures trade as participants reduced their holdings, in line with weak market trend.
Marketmen attributed the downward trend in guar seed futures to weak demand in spot market amid increasing supplies from growing regions.
On the National Commodity and Derivatives Exchange, guar seed contracts for January delivery fell by Rs 2, or 0.05 per cent, to Rs 4,230 per 10 quintal with an open interest of 52,420 lots.
Similarly, guar seed contracts for February delivery shed Re 1, or 0.03 per cent, to Rs 4,279 per 10 quintal with an open interest of 51,525 lots.
Mustard seed prices on Thursday dropped by Rs 29 to Rs 4,674 per quintal in futures trade as speculators cut down their positions on subdued demand.
On the National Commodity and Derivatives Exchange, mustard seed contracts for January delivery fell by Rs 29, or 0.62 per cent, to Rs 4,674 per quintal in a business turnover of 16,550 lots.
Mustard seed contracts for February delivery declined by Rs 31, or 0.66 per cent, to Rs 4,680 per quintal in a business turnover of 9,050 lots.
Marketmen said offloading of positions by participants following a weak trend in spot market led to fall in mustard seed prices.
Soya oil prices on Thursday fell by Rs 5 to Rs 938.6 per 10 kg in futures trade as speculators reduced holdings amid subdued demand in spot market.
On the National Commodity and Derivatives Exchange, refined soya oil for delivery in January declined by Rs 5, or 0.53 per cent, to Rs 938.6 per 10 kg in 34,080 lots.
The refined soya oil contracts for February dropped by Rs 4.6, or 0.49 per cent, to Rs 936.4 per 10 kg in 55,610 lots.
Market analysts said cutting down of positions by participants against ample stocks mainly influenced prices.
Soybean prices on Thursday fell by Rs 40 to Rs 4,416 per quintal in futures market as traders cut down their positions amid weak cues from physical markets.
On the National Commodity and Derivatives Exchange, soybean to be delivered in January contracts moved down by Rs 40, or 0.90 per cent, to Rs 4,416 per quintal with an open interest of 1,01,605 lots.
Soybean for delivery in February contracts also eased by Rs 70, or 1.56 per cent, to Rs 4,412 per quintal with an open interest of 1,71,630 lots.
The fall in soybean prices in futures trade was mostly due to reduction of exposure by participants, marketmen said.
