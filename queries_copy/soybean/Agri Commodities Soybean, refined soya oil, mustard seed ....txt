New Delhi: Soybean prices on Monday fell 3.58 per cent to Rs 3,658 per quintal in futures market as participants cut down their positions on weak spot demand.
On the National Commodity and Derivatives Exchange, soybean to be delivered in March contracts moved down by Rs 136, or 3.58 per cent, to Rs 3,658 per quintal with an open interest of 64,145 lots.
Soybean for delivery in May contracts also eased by Rs 142, or 3.8 per cent, to Rs 3,596 per quintal with an open interest of 63,990 lots.
The fall in soybean prices in futures trade was mostly due to reduction of exposure by participants, marketmen said.
Refined soya oil prices on Monday fell Rs 23.2 to Rs 770 per 10 kg in futures trade as speculators reduced holdings amid subdued demand in spot market.
On the National Commodity and Derivatives Exchange, refined soya oil for delivery in March declined by Rs 23.2, or 2.92 per cent, to Rs 770 per 10 kg in 21,720 lots.
The refined soya oil contracts for April dropped by Rs 25.8, or 3.32 per cent, to Rs 751.4 per 10 kg in 31,665 lots.
Market analysts said cutting down of positions by participants against ample stocks mainly influenced refined soya oil prices.
Mustard seed prices on Monday dropped by Rs 97 to Rs 3,990 per quintal in futures trade as participants reduced their holdings on sluggish demand.
On the National Commodity and Derivatives Exchange, mustard seed contracts for April delivery fell by Rs 97, or 2.37 per cent, to Rs 3,990 per quintal in a business turnover of 16,470 lots.
Mustard seed contracts for delivery in May declined by Rs 109, or 2.66 per cent, to Rs 3,988 per quintal in a business turnover of 6,480 lots.
Marketmen said offloading of positions by participants following a weak trend in spot market mainly led to fall in mustard seed prices.
Guar seed prices on Monday fell by Rs 150 to Rs 3,630 per 10 quintal in futures trade as participants reduced their holdings in line with a weak spot demand.
Marketmen attributed the downward trend in guar seed prices to increasing supplies from growing regions.
On the National Commodity and Derivatives Exchange, guar seed contracts for March delivery fell by Rs 150, or 3.97 per cent, to Rs 3,630 per 10 quintal with an open interest of 27,230 lots.
Similarly, guar seed contracts for April delivery shed Rs 150, or 3.99 per cent, to Rs 3,610 per 10 quintal with an open interest of 61,630 lots.
Guar gum prices on Monday plunged Rs 249 to Rs 5,988 per five quintal in futures market as traders offloaded their holdings in line with weak trend in spot market.
On the National Commodity and Derivatives Exchange, guar gum for March delivery fell by Rs 249, or 3.99 per cent, to Rs 5,988 per five quintal in 8,660 lots.
Guar gum for April delivery eased by Rs 252, or 4 per cent, to Rs 6,052 per five quintal in 48,680 lots.
Marketmen said trimming of positions by participants amid weak demand mainly dragged down guar gum prices here.
Cottonseed oil cake prices on Monday fell Rs 75 to Rs 1,803 per quintal in futures trade as participants reduced their bets tracking a weak trend in spot market.
Market players said sell-off by participants at existing levels amid subdued trend in market mainly weighed on cottonseed oil cake prices.
On the National Commodity and Derivatives Exchange, cottonseed oil cake to be delivered in March fell by Rs 75, or 3.99 per cent, to Rs 1,803 per quintal with an open interest of 34,660 lots.
Cottonseed oil cake for delivery in April fell by similar margins to Rs 1,813 per quintal in 57,220 lots.
Coriander prices on Monday fell by Rs 211 to Rs 6,013 per quintal in futures trade as speculators reduced their positions amid weak demand in spot market.
On the National Commodity and Derivatives Exchange, coriander contracts for April eased by Rs 211, or 3.39 per cent, to Rs 6,013 per quintal in 6,570 lots.
Similarly, the spice for May delivery plunged by Rs 230, or 3.69 per cent, at Rs 6,007 per quintal in 1,900 lots.
Market analysts said subdued demand in spot market mainly led to decline in coriander prices here.
