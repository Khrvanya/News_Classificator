Coriander prices on Tuesday rose by Rs 5 to Rs 5,850 per quintal in futures trade on firm demand at spot market.
On the National Commodity and Derivatives Exchange , coriander for April delivery shot up by Rs 5, or 0.09 per cent, to Rs 5,850 per quintal with an open interest of 7,030 lots.
A firm trend in spot market and restricted supplies from producing regions pushed coriander prices up, market analysts.
Soybean prices on Tuesday rose by Rs 30 to Rs 3,688 per quintal in futures market as traders created fresh positions in line with spot demand.
On the National Commodity and Derivatives Exchange, soybean for March delivery rose by Rs 30, or 0.82 per cent, to Rs 3,688 per quintal with an open interest of 90,595 lots.
Soybean for April delivery gained Rs 30, or 0.83 per cent, to Rs 3,662 per quintal with an open interest of 1,10,770 lots.
Market players said raising of fresh positions by speculators on the back of higher demand mainly led to rise in soybean prices.
Refined soya oil prices on Tuesday gained 0.82 per cent to Rs 764 per 10 kg in futures trade after speculators widened their holdings in line with positive spot demand.
On the National Commodity and Derivatives Exchange, refined soya oil for March delivery moved up by Rs 6.2, or 0.82 per cent, to Rs 764 per 10 kg in 32,340 lots.
Similarly, refined soya oil for April delivery edged up by Rs 5, or 0.67 per cent, to Rs 755.6 per 10 kg in 28,595 lots.
Analysts said widening of positions by traders mainly helped refined soya oil prices to trade higher in futures market here.
Mustard seed prices on Tuesday rose by Rs 8 to Rs 3,952 per quintal in futures trade as speculators raised their bets amid spot demand.
Marketmen said apart from restricted arrivals from growing belts demand from oil mills in the spot market mainly lifted mustard seed prices here.
On the National Commodity and Derivatives Exchange, mustard seed for delivery in April rose by Rs 8, or 0.20 per cent, to Rs 3,952 per quintal with an open interest of 19,000 lots.
Similarly, the delivery for May gained Rs 10, or 0.25 per cent, to Rs 3,979 per quintal with an open interest of 4,770 lots.
Guar seed prices on Tuesday rose by Rs 110 to Rs 3,720 per 10 quintal in futures trade after speculators increased their positions driven by a firm trend at spot market.
On the National Commodity and Derivatives Exchange, guar seed for March contracts increased by Rs 110, or 3.05 per cent, to Rs 3,720 per 10 quintal with an open interest of 42,200 lots.
Similarly, guar seed for April delivery gained Rs 106, or 2.93 per cent, to Rs 3,722 per 10 quintal with an open interest of 53,205 lots.
According to marketmen, raising of bets by speculators tracking a firm trend in spot market on thin supplies from growing belts mainly led to rise in guar seed prices here.
Guar gum prices on Tuesday surged Rs 207 to Rs 6,124 per five quintal in futures trade as speculators widened their holdings driven by firm trend at spot market.
On the National Commodity and Derivatives Exchange, guar gum for March delivery traded higher 3.5 per cent, or Rs 207, to Rs 6,124 per five quintal with an open interest of 16,625 lots.
Likewise, the contracts for April delivery was trading up by Rs 61, or 3.3 per cent, to Rs 6,193 per five quintal as open interest stood at 42,695 lots.
Analysts said after tracking firm physical market trend, traders raised their bets which led to rise in guar gum prices.
Cottonseed oil cake prices on Tuesday traded up by Rs 59 to Rs 1,667 per quintal in futures trade as participants enlargened their positions in line with positive spot demand.
On the National Commodity and Derivatives Exchange, cottonseed oil cake for delivery in March traded higher by Rs 59, or 3.67 per cent, to Rs 1,667 per quintal with an open interest of 71,560 lots.
Likewise for delivery in April, cottonseed oil cake traded up by Rs 60, or 3.69 per cent, to Rs 1,688 per quintal with an open interest of 48,170 lots.
Marketmen said raising of positions by participants amid a rising demand from cattle-feed makers mainly influenced cottonseed oil cake prices here.
