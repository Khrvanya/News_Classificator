Copper futures on Tuesday traded 0.67 per cent lower at Rs 432.50 per kg.
Nickel prices on Tuesday fell by Rs 6.20 to Rs 948.70 per kg in futures trade due to reducing of positions by speculators amid sluggish demand at spot market.
On the Multi Commodity Exchange, nickel contracts for February delivery fell by Rs 6.20, or 0.65 per cent, to Rs 948.70 per kg with a business turnover of 4,434 lots.
Similarly, nickel contracts for March delivery dropped by Rs 5, or 0.52 per cent, to Rs 950.50 per kg in 112 lots.
Analysts said subdued demand at spot market mainly led to fall in nickel prices here.
Copper futures on Tuesday traded 0.67 per cent lower at Rs 432.50 per kg as speculators reduced their exposure on weak spot demand.
On the Multi Commodity Exchange, copper contracts for February delivery fell by Rs 2.90, or 0.67 per cent, to Rs 432.50 per kg in a business turnover of 2,914 lots.
Similarly, copper contracts for March delivery traded lower by Rs 2.35 or 0.54 per cent, at Rs 436.10 per kg in a business turnover of 163 lots.
Traders said subdued demand in spot market mainly pushed down copper prices here.
