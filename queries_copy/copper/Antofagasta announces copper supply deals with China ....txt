China’s two copper smelters, Jiangxi Copper and Tongling Nonferrous, have reportedly inked copper concentrate supply deals with Antofagasta ( OTC:ANFGF ) for the first half of 2021.
Miners pay treatment and refining charges (TC/RCs) to smelters to turn ore into refined metal, and according to the sources the treatment charge (TC) was $60.8 per tonne
Spot TCs in China AM-CN-CUCONC are currently languishing at $54.50 a tonne, the lowest since September 2012, according to an assessment by Asian Metal.
