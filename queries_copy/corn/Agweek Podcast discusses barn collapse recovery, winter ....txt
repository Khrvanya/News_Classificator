Agweek Podcast discusses barn collapse recovery, winter corn harvest, sunflowers and the history of sugar beets
Guest Mohamed Khan talks going from Guyana to North Dakota and the history of sugar beets.
Mar 18th 2020 - 3pm.
This week's episode of the Agweek Podcast features a farm family thriving after their barn collapsed, updates on this winter's corn combining and why some farmers are considering adding or keeping sunflowers in their crop rotation. Our guest is Mohamed Khan, the Extension sugar beet specialist and professor at North Dakota State University, who shares a ton of great information on this Red River Valley crop, including some history on the plant.
