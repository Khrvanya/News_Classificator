Japan stocks lower at close of trade; Nikkei 225 down 0.74%
© Reuters.  Japan stocks lower at close of trade; Nikkei 225 down 0.74%
Investing.com – Japan stocks were lower after the close on Friday, as losses in the Paper & Pulp , Railway & Bus and Real Estate sectors led shares lower.
At the close in Tokyo, the Nikkei 225 fell 0.74%.
The best performers of the session on the Nikkei 225 were Yahoo Japan Corp. (T: 4689 ), which rose 4.30% or 19.0 points to trade at 461.0 at the close. Meanwhile, Toyobo Co., Ltd. (T: 3101 ) added 2.48% or 37.0 points to end at 1529.0 and Chugai Pharmaceutical Co., Ltd. (T: 4519 ) was up 2.46% or 390.0 points to 16245.0 in late trade.
The worst performers of the session were Tokyo Dome Corp. (T: 9681 ), which fell 5.73% or 50.0 points to trade at 822.0 at the close. Mitsui Engineering & Shipbuilding (T: 7003 ) declined 4.24% or 25.0 points to end at 565.0 and Yaskawa Electric Corp. (T: 6506 ) was down 3.99% or 155.0 points to 3730.0.
Falling stocks outnumbered advancing ones on the Tokyo Stock Exchange by 3050 to 586 and 78 ended unchanged.
The Nikkei Volatility , which measures the implied volatility of Nikkei 225 options, was unchanged 0% to 26.91.
Crude oil for July delivery was down 3.44% or 1.25 to $35.09 a barrel. Elsewhere in commodities trading, Brent oil for delivery in August fell 3.19% or 1.23 to hit $37.32 a barrel, while the August Gold Futures contract fell 0.10% or 1.75 to trade at $1738.05 a troy ounce.
USD/JPY was up 0.26% to 107.12, while EUR/JPY rose 0.38% to 121.17.
The US Dollar Index Futures was down 0.04% at 96.705.
